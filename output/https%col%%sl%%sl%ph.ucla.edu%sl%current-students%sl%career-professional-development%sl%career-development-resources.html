Career Development Resources

The FSPH Career and Professional Development Office is excited to empower our students and recent alumni with up to date employment information and career planning tools at every phase of their professional journey. In addition to the resources on our website, enrolled students have access to the FSPH Career and Professional Development Office Common Collaboration and Learning Environment (CCLE) course page to access exclusive resources for career exploration, internship or job searching, effective interviewing and salary negotiation as well as how to make an impact in the first 90 days on the job. This resource features information on upcoming workshops, career fairs, alumni networking events, and allows FSPH students to join discussions with one another while working towards similar career goals. In addition, all 10 recorded seminars from the 2016-2017 FSPH Professional Communication Skills Series are available through CCLE.

The UCLA Fielding School of Public Health Career and Professional Development Office Facebook page is designed to provide students a common social media space to learn of internships, fellowships and job opportunities, upcoming workshops, career fairs, alumni networking events, and join discussions with other FSPH students and alumni in diverse public health practice areas. 

Additional resources for FSPH alumni 

Alumni Professional Development listserv
The Fielding School of Public Health Offices of Career and Professional Development and External Affairs has created a new Alumni Professional Development listserv to inform alumni of job, fellowship, professional development, and FSPH engagement opportunities. To stay connected, please subscribe to the listserv by visiting this group at https://groups.google.com/a/lists.ucla.edu/d/forum/fsph_alumniprofdev and request to join with your full name listed. 

Once subscribed, please feel free to send announcements for sharing regarding public health alumni jobs, fellowships, networking opportunities, or professional development events to Fsph_alumniprofdev@lists.ucla.edu.

To unsubscribe from this group, send an email to fsph_alumniprofdev+unsubscribe@lists.ucla.edu

Visit this group at https://groups.google.com/a/lists.ucla.edu/d/forum/fsph_alumniprofdev

** Please note this is a private, moderated listserv for FSPH alumni only. Please do not share with any non-FSPH alums.
