L.A. County Career Summit

Join us for the first L.A. County Career Summit to demystify the L.A. County hiring process and learn everything you need to know to start your career with them. Chat with the staff from different departments to learn about what they're looking for in terms of employees, the types of available positions and to ask questions. The event is Free and open to COC students, alumni and community members. 

Date & Time: 
Event took place on Wednesday 08/12/2020 - 9:00 am to 4:00 pm
