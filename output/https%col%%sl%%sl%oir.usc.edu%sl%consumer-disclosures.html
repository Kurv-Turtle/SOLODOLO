HEERF Quarterly Fund Report

The U.S. Department of Education published two Notices of Public Posting Requirement, on August 31, 2020 and May 13, 2021, directing universities to report on the receipt and use of funds received from the HEERF. Our most recent quarterly report on the student portion funds is now available. Our Quarterly Budget and Expenditure Report for the institutional portion funds is also available.
