HEERF Grant & CARES Act

HEERF Grant

The Higher Education Emergency Relief Funds III (HEERF 3.0) provides federal emergency financial aid assistance to students for expenses related to COVID-19 as stipulated by the U.S. American Rescue Plan was signed into law on March 11, 2021. HEERF 3.0 Emergency Relief Grant funding is one-time funding divided between undergraduate, graduate, and professional students based on the percentage of enrollment.

Information about eligibility and how to receive the Higher Education Emergency Relief Funds III (HEERF 3.0) can be found on the Financial Aid website.

  • View the HEERF FAQs
  • View the HEERF Reports

HEERF Grant 2.0

Higher Education Emergency Relief Funds 2.0 is no longer available. Any remaining HEERF 2.0 funding has been reallocated into HEERF 3.0 funds. Please refer back to Higher Education Emergency Relief Funds III (HEERF 3.0) FAQ’s. You can view the archived HEERF 2.0 information page here.

The CARES Act

CARES Act Emergency Relief Grant Fund is no longer available. Any remaining CARES funding has been reallocated into HEERF 2.0 funds. Please refer back to Higher Education Emergency Relief Funds II (HEERF 2.0) FAQs. You can find the archived CARES information here.

  • View the CARES Reports
