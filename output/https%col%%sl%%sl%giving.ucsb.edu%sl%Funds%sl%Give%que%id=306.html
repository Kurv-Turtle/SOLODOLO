Transfer Student Center Hide Details

UCSB's Transfer Student Center works to connect transfer students to resources that will help them thrive academically and socially, and prepare them for life beyond UC Santa Barbara. The Center helps students fully participate in the undergraduate experience by providing gathering and study space, academic advising, peer mentoring, specialized programming, and access to undergraduate research. Located in the University Library, the Center is at the physical and intellectual cross-roads of campus, creating a perfect cross-disciplinary environment for all to succeed.
