Reminder of COVID presence and value of masking

May 16, 2022

As fluctuating case numbers and circulating variants remind us, COVID has not gone away. As campus activities increase, masking, testing and other health protocols are crucial to our efforts to minimize COVID spread.

Update on masking guidelines

April 21, 2022

In light of an increase in COVID cases, Stanford continues to strongly encourage face coverings indoors and in crowded outdoor spaces. Masking will continue to be required in classrooms and on Marguerite buses until further notice.
