Facilities Services Serves the Campus Community
 

We provide services to the entire UCR campus community consisting of approximately 23,000 students, 1,100 institutional faculty, 6,000 staff and 6,800 on-campus housing residents. We are able to support the campus through the commitment, dedication and hard work by each of our employees every day. As experts in our fields, we also maintain buildings, facilities and landscapes totaling over:

7.7
million square feet of space
1,350
acres of campus
6
