Invasive & Exotic Pests

Invasive and exotic pests threaten California's natural environments, agricultural production, structures, landscapes and gardens. Learn more about the pests and diseases that are currently in California. Watch/lookout for pests that have a high likelihood of being detected in California in the near future. ().
