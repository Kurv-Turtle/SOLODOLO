CalArts Creates at LAPL: Summer 2022

 

Sign Up Here!

This program is presented by a partnership between the CalArts Community Arts Partnership (CAP) and the Los Angeles Public Library (LAPL). 

We invite you to sign up for our CalArts Creates at LAPL Summer Workshops! Sign up for a free four-week workshop in either Zine-Making or Digital Music (or sign up for both!) taught by CalArts alumni and students! Eligible participants must reside in LA County and be a current student between the ages of 12 and 18. Past experience is not required. 

PLEASE APPLY BY JUNE 21. SOME STUDENTS MAY BE PLACED ON THE WAITLIST.

All class meetings will take place in person at:
Los Angeles Central Library
630 W 5th St, Los Angeles, CA 90071
Classes are free. Classroom art supplies will be available for every student.

Workshop 1: Digital Music Composition
July 12 - August 4, 2022
Tuesdays and Thursdays (8 sessions)
10AM-12:30PM

Workshop 2: Zine-making
July 12 - August 4, 2022
Tuesdays and Thursdays (8 sessions)
1PM-3:30PM

Please send any questions to Program Manager Annapurna Kumar at akumar@calarts.edu

Self-Help Graphics

October - December 2019 & February - April 2020

In partnership with Self Help Graphics, CAP offers a free after-school visual arts class for high school youth participants in Boyle Heights. Classes are taught by CalArts alumni and current students. Classes will be held on Mondays from 4:15 - 7:15 PM from October-December and February-April, culminating in a final exhibition. All equipment and supplies are provided. Classes do not meet on holidays and breaks. 

To register, contact Self Help Graphics at 323-881-6444 or info@selfhelpgraphics.com.

For more information please contact Program Coordinator Annapurna Kumar at akumar@calarts.edu.

July 5 - July 30, 2021

CAPSA CREATIVE LABS 

These programs have concluded for Summer 2021. Please check back next year!

CalArts Community Arts Partnership Summer Arts (CAPSA) is offering free art classes for high school students in Los Angeles County! The program will be conducted remotely on Zoom. Register HERE!

Each class is a one week-long Creative Lab where students experiment with different fields of art making. The six Creative Labs are: Animation, Creative Writing, Film, Music, Theater, and Visual Arts. Students can participate in up to four labs or take just one. All six labs will run for one week beginning on a Monday and ending on a Friday.

For more program details, click here: https://www.calartscapsa.com/  

Please direct any questions to Program Manager Alma E. Catalán at acatalanmontiel@calarts.edu
