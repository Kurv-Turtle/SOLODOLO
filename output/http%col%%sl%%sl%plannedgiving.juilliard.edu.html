Make a Lasting Impact at Juilliard

Since Juilliard's founding in 1905, hundreds of generous individuals have supported future young artists with bequests and other planned gifts for the school. By including Juilliard in your long-term plans, you can provide critical assistance for the school—and for the musicians, dancers, and actors preparing for careers in the performing arts.

Please visit the links on this page, or contact Lori Padua at (212) 799-5000, ext. 7152, or at lpadua@juilliard.edu for more information about making a planned gift for The Juilliard School.
