President Michael V. Drake, M.D.

  • Overview
  • About
  • Public engagement
  • Contact

"The University of California has always had a policy of access, affordability and excellence."

Dr. Michael V. Drake is the 21st president of the University of California. He oversees UC's world-renowned university system of 10 campuses, five medical centers, three nationally affiliated labs, more than 280,000 students and 230,000 faculty and staff. Learn more about President Drake.
