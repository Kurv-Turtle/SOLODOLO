UH Mānoa » Students

As a UH Mānoa student you can choose from 99 Bachelor’s, 85 Master’s, and 58 Professional and Doctoral degrees.

We also encourage you to get involved outside the classroom and participate in some of the over 250 student organizations and enhanced learning opportunities available on campus.
