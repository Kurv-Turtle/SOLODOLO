Graduate Office for Student Rights and Responsibilities

The Office for Student Rights and Responsibilities assists students, faculty, and staff in matters related to graduate academic conduct by supporting departments and programs in developing and implementing academic conduct policies, and assisting students in navigation of such policies and procedures. The OSRR also supports Academic Student Employees, as well as departments and programs and Employee and Labor Relations to resolve UAW grievances, and to liaise with departments and employees with the aim of using administrative advocacy and cross-campus partnerships to reduce errors, misunderstandings, and system-related issues that lead to grievances by student employees.

The Graduate Division is committed to expanding the diversity of Berkeley’s student body, and supporting students from all backgrounds, especially those from underrepresented groups, in their academic, personal and professional journeys. We’re here to help you build community and make our campus your new home.
