» Meet our Argyros Ambassadors

We understand it's important to find a graduate business program right for you and your future career.   We are committed to recruiting high-achieving and diverse students, and our Argyros Ambassadors are here to answer your questions and provide insight on the Argyros experience.

Learn how to become an Argyros Ambassador by contacting mba@chapman.edu.

Undergraduate Degree: Bachelor of Arts, American Studies
Hometown: Cedar City, UT
E-mail: kewhitney@chapman.edu

Kevin was born and raised in southern Utah before moving to Las Vegas for high school. Upon graduation he yearned for travel and direction and enlisted in the United States Navy as a military police officer. Kevin was stationed in Washington state on a submarine base responsible for the protection and safe transportation of nuclear assets, where at nineteen years old was in the position of Waterfront Supervisor responsible for 30 plus Sailors. Kevin’s final two years in the Navy were spent stationed in the Mediterranean providing security for high level air assets. After four years in the Navy, Kevin was honorably discharged and began his education in Long Beach, CA at the community college, where soon after he transferred to California State University Long Beach. Kevin was on the president’s list for each semester he attended, graduated Magna Cum Laude, and was awarded Distinguish Graduate for his major in American Studies, with a Chemistry minor thrown. After spending 4 years working towards medical school, he realized that a life of medicine was not right for him and his family. Kevin has always been entrepreneurial minded, working since he was 11 when he started his own lawn and weed business, and so business seemed like the perfect fit. Shortly after graduating, Kevin became a licensed real estate professional, and started his own business in residential real estate and vacation rental property management. When Kevin is not showing homes, managing his properties, or playing with his baby, he is at Chapman University working on his MBA at night. Kevin lives in Lake Forest with his wife, daughter, and three dogs.
