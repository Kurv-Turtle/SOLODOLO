Perks

In addition to monetary incentives, BeWell participants also receive other perks, including free personal training, wellness grants and special events.

Perks for BeWell participantsEvents & Additional PerksRecreation Facility Information

Perks for BeWell participants

  • Free annual fitness assessment available through Recreation
  • Two free personal training sessions each year offered by Recreation
  • Free Stanford Athletics tickets to select sporting events. 
  • Wellness grants ($200 to $1,000) available to Champions to foster wellness within their workgroups
  • Up to $560 in taxable incentives

Events & Additional Perks

BeWell holds regular events throughout the year to give you a chance to learn more about your well-being and to take a healthy break during the day.

Free annual events:

  • Wellness Fair
  • Cardinal Walk
  • The Science and Application of Play
  • LeadWell for Life
  • Compassion Week

Well-time: Convert up to eight hours of your sick time each year to use for participating in BeWell. (Supervisor approval required.)

Recreation Facility Information

  • Free for all staff and spouses/partners with a Stanford ID
  • Spouses or partners may access with a Recreation Courtesy Card – Go to Tressider ID card office with spouse/partner, proof of joint residence, and $25 cash for card processing fee.
  • Guest passes available for $10 (must be 18+, with exception of family swim)
  • Day use lockers are available at all facilities
  • Quarterly and annual locker rentals available in AOERC and Ford Center
  • Check out equipment for free with your ID at the front desk
