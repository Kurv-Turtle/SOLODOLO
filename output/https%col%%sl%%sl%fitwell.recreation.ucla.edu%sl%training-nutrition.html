Training & Nutrition

Request A Trainer

(Virtual Personal Fitness Training Available)

*The program is currently at capacity. As of May 4, 2022, you will be applying for the personal fitness training waitlist.

Personal training is available remotely and in person. In-person sessions are available at the John Wooden Center, Kinross Recreation Center, Bruin Fitness Center, and Drake Stadium. Location will be determined based on client goals and facility availability.

Get Started

Step 1: Complete an application. Read and complete the following forms:

  • Participant Information
  • Pre-participation Screening
  • Physician’s Release (Download this form if needed based on answers to Pre-participation Screening)

Step 2:Purchase Sessions - Personal Training, Private Pilates, Private Yoga

Purchase session(s) online prior to your first appointment. Please forward the receipt indicating proof of purchase to your trainer.

Step 3: Schedule training sessions. A FITWELL Coordinator will contact you within three business days to connect you with a trainer.

Policies

  • All required forms must be completed, current and on file prior to each session.
  • All forms expire 12 months from completion date or as medical history changes.
  • All clients must wear appropriate footwear and clothing to each session.

*Personal Fitness Training on the premises of any UCLA Recreation facility is restricted to UCLA Recreation Personal Fitness Trainers (PFT) only. Any participant who is conducting or is suspected of conducting personal fitness training with a patron in a UCLA Recreation facility and who is not a UCLA Recreation PFT will have membership privileges reviewed and/or revoked.
