Student Life

CSUCI’s campus: Stunning. Your life here: Incredible. CSUCI’s beautiful campus in Camarillo is midway between Santa Barbara and Los Angeles and just 25 miles from Malibu. We’re nestled against the foothills of the Santa Monica Mountains—and not far from the Pacific Ocean.

No Ferry Required. Despite our name, CSUCI is surrounded by lemon groves and strawberry and raspberry fields. With Spanish-style buildings and courtyards, our peaceful campus is an ideal place for you to immerse yourself in your studies.

Off-Campus Fun

With our great location, off-campus fun is just minutes away. Via bike, bus or 24/7 access to Zipcars, you can head to nearby beaches, parks, bike paths and hiking trails, cultural opportunities, and shopping—or get to:

  • Los Angeles (57 miles)
  • Malibu (25 miles)
  • Santa Barbara (47 miles)
