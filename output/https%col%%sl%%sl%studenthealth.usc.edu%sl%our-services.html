Our Services

USC Student Health is your source for comprehensive healthcare on both USC’s University Park Campus (UPC) and Health Sciences Campus (HSC). Our services are covered by your mandatory Student Health Fee each semester, and our facilities on both campuses are certified by the Accreditation Association for Ambulatory Health Care (AAAHC).

All of our physicians are board certified with clinical faculty appointments in the Keck School of Medicine at USC. Our team is dedicated to providing all of your basic primary care needs, mental health and counseling services, and a variety of health and well-being activities through campus wide partnerships. We also offer on-site specialty care services such as allergy, immunization clinics, and dermatology.
