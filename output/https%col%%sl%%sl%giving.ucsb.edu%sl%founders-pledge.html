Innovators Circle

Welcome to the Innovators Circle at UC Santa Barbara – your campus-wide gateway to a community of entrepreneurs and innovators who collectively pool their talent, networks, and capital to support one another, the university, and an ecosystem dedicated to elevating innovation.

In partnership with campus and corporate participants from UC Santa Barbara's Innovation + Entrepreneurship, members gain access to lectures and webinars from some of our most brilliant minds, diverse talent pools, ideas, networking receptions, mentorship opportunities, student start-up competitions, and much more.

As your business evolve, you will have a network of university resources for recruitment, visibility, peer connection, as well as startup support and help you discover the areas you are most passionate about at UC Santa Barbara. By joining this group, you are helping to build a culture of giving back and will inspire others to do the same.

We look forward to welcoming you to the Innovators Circle.

Join the Innovators Circle Today

If you are interested in joining the Innovators Circle at UC Santa Barbara, please contact Mehgan Tanner at mehgan.tanner@ucsb.edu or (805) 893-5650.

The next exciting chapter in your entrepreneurial journey awaits and joining is simple. Make your Founders' Pledge – a personal, non-binding pledge to philanthropically support the UC Santa Barbara at a meaningful level – and let the journey begin.

Make the Founders' Pledge.

Frequently Asked Questions

The University will organize programs with meaningful and relevant content and dialogues to augment these connections and conversations.
