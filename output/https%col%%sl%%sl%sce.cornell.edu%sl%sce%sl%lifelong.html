Lifelong Learners

Take a Cornell course on campus or online, develop your professional skills, or enjoy an education vacation.

Take a Cornell course for credit, on campus or online

Continue your education, further your career, or simply have fun learning something new by taking a Cornell course. You can choose from thousands of regular university classes year-round and earn credit and a Cornell transcript. Enrollment is open to all.

Find a course for credit

Visit a course at a 90% discount

The Visitors' Noncredit Program allows adults who are not Cornell University degree candidates to sit in on most regularly scheduled, non-participatory, on-campus courses. It's a great way to take advantage of Cornell's extensive course offerings.

Advance your career, develop or hone your skills

Work with Cornell experts to increase your capabilities through our professional studies programs, internships, online classes, and part-time study. Drawn from across the university, these dynamic faculty members understand how to address your needs in a rapidly changing environment.

Explore program options

Travel the world with Cornell faculty

Cornell's Adult University (CAU) offers inspiring summer programs on the Cornell campus and unforgettable travel adventures to spectacular destinations around the world, all designed and led by top-notch Cornell faculty. These exciting, Ivy League education vacations are open to everyone, including Cornell alumni, families, and children.
