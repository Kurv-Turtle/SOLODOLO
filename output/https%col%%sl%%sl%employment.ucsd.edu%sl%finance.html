Finance

A career worth earning.

Advance Together

The financial sustainability of UC San Diego and UC San Diego Health depends on the hundreds of finance professionals that support our academic, research and clinical missions. Finance-related career opportunities exist throughout our institution. Of special interest, UC San Diego hosts a research enterprise worth 1.5 billion dollar annually. It ranks 6th in the nation for research and 3rd for start-ups. As a top-ranking, future oriented organization, we offer challenging Financial Analysis and Fund Management career opportunities in a fast-paced and innovative environment.

Here’s why people love working at
UC San Diego

From COVID-19 to The U.S. Agency for International Development (USAID), my job ensures researchers have the funds to make a difference in the world – and that is immensely gratifying.

Sam, Associate Director, Sponsored Projects

Our team is more than just a group of peers - they are my work family. I have made lasting relationships here and have a supportive community among the people I work with every day.

Nirvana, Financial Analyst
