Cooking Well

Participants of all skill levels are welcome to the SRC cooking demonstrations, located in the Classroom Kitchen. Our kitchen is fully equipped with the tools necessary for our chef to improve your personal wellness through any of our healthy cooking classes. With the help of Chef Val, you will pick up valuable cooking techniques. Come, eat, and go home with delicious healthy recipes.

SPECIAL ANNOUNCEMENT: Hi Highlanders! Chef Val here and happy to be back. I am pleased to announce that the Cooking Well program will resume programming this Fall 2022. In the meantime, keep an eye out for our Cooking Well Assistant job openings. I can’t wait to reconnect with all of you where we’ll be Cooking Well and eating better.

- Chef Val
