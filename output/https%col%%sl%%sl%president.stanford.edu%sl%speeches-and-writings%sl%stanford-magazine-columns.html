The Stanford Fund Partnership Program: On-Campus Funding for Student Activities

The Stanford Fund Partnership Program is a unique arrangement between The Stanford Fund, the Office of Student Engagement, and hundreds of Stanford's undergraduate student groups. It is designed to supplement other on-campus funding in lieu of students fundraising externally.
