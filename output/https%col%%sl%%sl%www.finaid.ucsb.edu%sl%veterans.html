Thank you for your service, and welcome UCSB Veterans, Service Members and Military Dependents! The VA Certifying Official in the Office of Financial Aid and Scholarships is responsible for certifying VA educational benefits and for assisting veterans and dependents with their financial aid. Also check out the UCSB Veteran Resource Center for additional veteran programs and resources. 

Questions? Schedule a call with the VA Certifying Official

Go here to submit your electronic Veteran Benefits Request Form. Please do not send Veteran Benefits Request Forms through e-mail.
