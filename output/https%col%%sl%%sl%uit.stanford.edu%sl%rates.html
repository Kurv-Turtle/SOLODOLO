Rates

We provide more than 100 technology services to support the activities of faculty, staff, students, residents, and visitors at Stanford and its affiliated hospitals and clinics.

While some of our services are provided at no charge, many are offered for a fee. The fees we collect cover our costs and overhead expenses associated with service delivery. That said, we are committed to providing the highest value services at the lowest cost possible.

Determining rates

Many factors impact the rates we charge, causing increases or decreases accordingly. These factors include:

  • Advances in technology
  • Demand for and use of our services
  • Annual staff salary increases
  • Licensing and software subscription fees
  • Operational improvements such as automation

Visit this webpage for more information on FY23 rate changes that are effective Sept. 1, 2022, or browse our current rates by service type on the sidebar to the right.
