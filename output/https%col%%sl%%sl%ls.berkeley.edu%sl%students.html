For Students

Background image: Students gathering on lawn

Every year, 6,000 L&S students graduate ready to engage effectively and courageously with the challenges of our time. Big place?  Sure, but with big options for finding close communities, including discussion sections, study groups, seminars, Berkeley Connect programs, and research supervised by faculty members. Undergraduates can also choose among hundreds of student organizations, getting together with people who share their passions — for the arts, social activism, sports, exploration of our beautiful natural setting, and much more.
