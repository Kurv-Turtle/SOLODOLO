Strategic Planning

UCR is currently engaged in strategic planning and The Office of the Provost manages a separate website (strategicplan.ucr.edu) that provides information on the planning process, how to get involved, and the interim outcomes of strategic planning.

In brief, the strategic planning process is based on the work of committees with representatives from across campus. Committees explore a variety of topics related to strategic planning in higher education by engaging deeply with the community and analyzing input from stakeholders. Each committee will write a report on their topic that considers a set of cross-cutting themes. The Strategic Planning Committee will pull together all committee reports and public feedback to draft the first full version of the Strategic Plan. The first draft will also be posted for public feedback before being published in its final form. For more detail on this process and timeline visit strategicplan.ucr.edu/process-and-timeline.
