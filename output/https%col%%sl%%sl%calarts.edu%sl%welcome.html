Your Next Steps

To enroll at CalArts, you'll need to take care of a few more things. You may need to log in to your CalArts SSO account in order to complete these items. You can find your temporary CalArts Credentials at apply.calarts.edu/apply/status, and login at sso.calarts.edu to reset your temporary password. If you need help, you can consult this guide for new students.

Enrollment Deposit and Decision Reply Form

If you intend to accept your offer of admission and enroll at CalArts, you will need to submit your non-refundable $700 enrollment deposit and your decision reply form by the deadline stated on your admission letter. To do so, log in to the Application Portal. Contact your admission representative for more questions. 

Access to CalArts Email Account

After you place your tuition deposit and submit your decision reply form, you will be assigned your CalArts email. 

Housing Deposit & Application

The Housing Application is due on May 20. Apply through your SSO Account after you place your tuition deposit and are assigned your CalArts email. Click on "Symplicity - Residence" to find the application. A $350 deposit is required in order to apply. Please see our Residential Life site for more information on our housing options and how to apply.

Please note that proof of Meningitis vaccination must be submitted before being allowed to move into campus Residential spaces. See below under "Immunization Forms" for more information.

Transcript Requirements, Transfer Credit, and Credit by Examination

If you plan to attend CalArts, please submit official final transcripts and/or test scores to the Office of Admissions. 

Click here to learn more how to complete your submission.

Immunization Forms

In an effort to keep our community healthy, all students are required to have an immunization record on file with the Student Health Center which includes MMR, Tuberculosis, and Meningitis vaccinations. Immunization records are submitted through our HIPAA compliant platform, Medicat, located on your CalArts SSO account. Meningitis proof must be submitted before being allowed to move into campus Residential spaces. For more information, visit our Health Services section.

Health Insurance Requirement

Click here for more information on health insurance requirements.

Please note that travel insurance does not meet CalArts Student Health Insurance Plan (SHIP) requirements.

Setup Self-Service Proxy Access

You can designate one or more individuals as authorized parties to have access to your FERPA protected information such as financial aid, student accounts, and registration information. To learn more and set up Proxy access please click here.

Course Registration

New Student Registration opens on August 16. Information on the registration process will be sent by email in early summer. 

International Students Next Steps

Your first step towards attending CalArts is to submit your tuition deposit and decision reply form at apply.calarts.edu/apply/status. 

A few days after you pay the tuition deposit, you will receive information by email about the student visa application process. For an F-1 visa, you will be required to verify that you have sufficient funds to meet tuition and living expenses while studying in the United States.
