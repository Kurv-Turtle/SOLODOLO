Our Unifying Values

At USC our six unifying values support our University’s mission, and guide our behaviors, the way we engage with ourselves and our community, to create our Trojan culture.

Our Values Are:

  • Integrity
  • Well-being
  • Excellence
  • Open Communication
  • Diversity, Equity, and Inclusion
  • Accountability

Learn more about the Unifying Values

Culture is a Shared Commitment

When we embrace these values together as faculty, staff, and students we build a culture that instills trust, reinforces ethical decision-making, and provides a strong foundation as we boldly advance into the future.
