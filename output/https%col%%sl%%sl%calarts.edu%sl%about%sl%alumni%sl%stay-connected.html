What can you do on CalArts Connect?

Connect and Re-Connect

Utilize the trusted CalArts environment to expand your network of collaborators and re-connect with old classmates.

Give back

Introduce, employ, and offer to act as a mentor to our graduating students.

Expand

Leverage your professional network to get introduced to people you should know.
