BHIP for Staff & Faculty

BHIP .5

Bruin Health Improvement Program (BHIP) .5: Physical Activity, Nutrition, & Healthy Cooking

The Bruin Health Improvement Program (BHIP) .5 is a 10 week long lifestyle change program designed to increase the fitness and thereby the health of the UCLA Employee. BHIP .5 is a customized program for those who are sedentary and looking to lose weight. It is funded by UCLA and is a tremendous investment in UCLA’s most precious resource: the staff and faculty.

**Must be employed 50% time or more to be eligible to apply for BHIP and/or BHIP.5.

For the latest updates and insight into our community, like us on Facebook

Virtual BHIP.5 Programs - Spring 2022:

NUTRITION & HEALTHY COOKING PROGRAM

A 10-week lifestyle change program that offers comprehensive nutritional education and coaching, live practical cooking classes, meal plans, and weekly nutrition challenges. One live nutrition/cooking/meal prepping session led by the UCLA Teaching Kitchen chef and FITWELL registered dietitian and one live nutrition class are offered each week. BHIP.5 Nutrition Only is appropriate for individuals looking to lose weight, improve their cooking skills & learn to meal prep, develop healthier, more mindful heating habits, and improve overall health. Participants are chosen via a lottery drawing of applications. Click on the link below to apply to the lottery!

Not offered for Spring 2022.

PHYSICAL ACTIVITY, NUTRITION & HEALTHY COOKING PROGRAM

A 10-week lifestyle change program that offers low-impact physical activity, live practical cooking classes, nutrition education, meal plans, and weekly challenges. Three live workouts led by certified fitness professionals and one live nutrition/cooking/meal prepping session led by the UCLA Teaching Kitchen chef and FITWELL registered dietitian are offered each week. BHIP.5 is appropriate for individuals who are sedentary or whose primary goals may be to lose weight, increase daily physical activity, enhance cooking skills & learn to meal prep, develop healthier, more mindful eating habits, and improve overall health. Participants are chosen via a lottery drawing of applications. Click on the link below to apply to the lottery!

BHIP OnRamp

Bruin Health Improvement Program (BHIP) Onramp: Strength and Conditioning

The Bruin Health Improvement Program (BHIP) is a 12 week long program—exclusively for UCLA faculty and staff at no cost (for first time participants)—offering participants intensive lifestyle training in areas of exercise, nutrition, stress management and mental conditioning.

BHIP OnRamp is a comprehensive conditioning program that will improve cardiovascular fitness and overall strength and mobility. It is appropriate for individuals without current limitations looking to become much more physically active and nutritionally mindful.

* *Must be employed 50% time or more to be eligible to apply for BHIP and/or BHIP.5.

BHIP OnRamp - Spring 2022:

A 12-week health improvement program designed for individuals looking to learn proper exercise technique, start/improve a regular resistance training program, and increase strength & overall fitness. Enjoy live workouts led by certified fitness professionals three times per week and improve eating habits with a 30 Day Nutrition Challenge. BHIP is appropriate for individuals who are generally active, but looking to add regular resistance training, and create sustainable nutrition habits to help reach their overall health goals. A virtual class time is offered for those who prefer to participate virtually via Zoom. BHIP OnRamp feeds into the BHIP Ongoing Program. Participants are chosen via a lottery drawing of applications.

Program Dates: 5/23/22 - 8/12/22

Class Days : Monday, Wednesday, & Friday
