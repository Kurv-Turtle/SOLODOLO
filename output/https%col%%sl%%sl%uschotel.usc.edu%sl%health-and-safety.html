Health and
Safety Protocols

USC Hotel wants you to Travel Confidently

Rest assured that we are taking the necessary steps to provide a safe and comfortable environment for everyone. In accordance with guidelines from the USC Department of Health & Safety, the City of Los Angeles, and the California Hotel & Lodging Association (CHLA), USC Hotel has implemented protocols to ensure our guests’ health and peace of mind while staying with us. From the use of face coverings to enhanced sanitation and cleaning procedures, we are committed to keeping our guests, staff, and community safe.

SAFETY HIGHLIGHTS

Amenity Care Kits

Health & Safety kits, available upon request.

Elevator Safety

Increased frequency of sanitizing.

Hand Sanitizer

Contactless sanitizer stations located throughout property.

PPE

Mask or face coverings are required for everyone regardless of vaccination status for COVID-19.

Vaccine Requirements

The City of LA is requiring proof of COVID vaccination or negative COVID test taken within 72 hours to enter our hotel restaurants and fitness center.

We are proud to announce our "Clean + Safe" Certification with the California Hotel & Lodging Association (CHLA.) This will allow us to provide the same USC Hotel hospitality you have come to count on while giving our staff and guests the utmost peace of mind. The CHLA "Clean + Safe" Lodging Certification establishes cleanliness best practices and provides a unified standard for safe business operations.
