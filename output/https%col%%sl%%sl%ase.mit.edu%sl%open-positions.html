Atomistic Simulation & Energy Research Group

Open Positions

The ASE research group consists of a team of nominally 5-15 members that are an extremely talented and hard working combination of graduate students (masters and Ph.D.), postdocs as well as research engineers and research scientists. As noted, open positions come available from time to time based on the availability of funding and the current group composition. If you are interested in possibly joining the group, please follow the link to the specific position(s) you are interested and follow the detailed instructions for how to submit an application.
