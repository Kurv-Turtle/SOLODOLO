Shaurjya Banerjee – The Death Cube

by wavecave
April 11, 2016
2016
  • Tweet
  • Pin It

The Death Cube is a large scale steel kinetic sculpture that attempts to connect materials, movements and tangibility.

Shaurjya Banerjee is a creative technologist and kinetic sculptor from Calcutta, India. 

The Death Cube was installed in the WaveCave from April 11th to April 18th, 2016
