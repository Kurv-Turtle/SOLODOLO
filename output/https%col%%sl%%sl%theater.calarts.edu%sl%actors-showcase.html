Actors Showcase

As part of the transition from their training to the professional world, the graduating BFA and MFA actors present their annual showcase in New York and Los Angeles.  

New York Showcase

Friday, April 8, 2022, ‍at 5 pm
Five Angels Theater
789 Tenth Ave., New York, NY 

Los Angeles Showcase

Wednesday, April 27, 2022, ‍at 7 pm
The Colony Theatre
555 N. Third St. Burbank, CA

More information

The showcase affords students the opportunity to perform before industry professionals— including agents, managers, casting directors, producers, directors, and various creative professionals—presenting scenes and monologues from both published and original works. The work is also shot, edited, and presented online to maximize the visibility of our graduates' wide-range of talents and singular artistry.

Voiceover Showcase

The CalArts School of Theater is thrilled to be the first actor training program in the country to have a fully realized Voice Over Showcase.
