Call for Works – Fall 2022

CalArts Music School students are invited to submit proposals for sound-related installations to be programmed in the WaveCave gallery during the Fall 2022 semester. The WaveCave will be available for week-long shows, Sundays through Saturdays throughout most of the Fall 2022 semester.

Dates

Proposals are due by Friday, May 6, 2022 at 5pm
Selection Notifications will be sent by Monday, May 16, 2022
Commitment to assigned gallery dates is due by Monday, May 23, 2022

(Notification emails will be sent for accepted proposals only)

Criteria

Proposals will be reviewed by a committee consisting of CalArts Music School  faculty and will be evaluated based on the following criteria:

  • Clarity of the proposal
  • Interesting usage of sound within the gallery space
  • Experimentation in process or approach; or challenge to formal conventions
  • Feasibility of the proposal

Proposal Guidelines

Create and upload a PDF with the following information:

  1. Synopsis of the Piece (50 to 100 words) - What exactly will we see and hear as we enter the gallery and explore the work
  2. Detailed Description of the Piece (500 words or less) - Tell us more about your approach both aesthetically and technically
  3. A diagram of the physical layout of the piece in the gallery
  4. A system diagram of any electronics
  5. Pertinent links to your current / previous work that may help us understand the work you are proposing
  6. Bio (200 words or less)

Proposal Form
