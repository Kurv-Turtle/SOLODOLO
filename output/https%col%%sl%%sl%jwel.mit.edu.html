J-WEL Collaboratives

 

J-WEL pK-12 brings to life MIT's unique "mens et manus" learning approach through experiences that combine hands-on project-based learning and the pursuit of learning progressions centered on foundational concepts in pre-Kindergarten through 12th grade.


J-WEL pK-12



J-WEL Higher Education has a visionary and outcome-driven approach. J-WEL members address key educational goals by carrying out projects with MIT/J-WEL input.


J-WEL Higher Education



J-WEL Workforce Learning provides its members a forum to learn from, and in some cases participate in, research projects designed to improve workplace learning effectiveness.


J-WEL Workforce Learning
