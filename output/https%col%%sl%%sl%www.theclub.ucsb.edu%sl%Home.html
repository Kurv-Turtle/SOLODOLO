Welcome

The Club & Guest House welcomes guests to dine, stay, meet and gather in an architectural icon—newly renovated and expanded—in the heart of the UC Santa Barbara campus. Our facility offers an all-inclusive guest experience with fine-dining luncheon settings, a 34-room, boutique hotel-style Guest House, private meeting areas, state-of-the-art conference spaces, outdoor terrace for special occasions and on-site catering. Boasting a modernized design by acclaimed architects Moore Ruble Yudell, we are proud to have achieved LEED silver certification for our sustainable and energy-efficient construction. We are pleased to welcome a variety of guests, maintaining a long-standing tradition of conversation, connection and celebration since our opening in 1968.
