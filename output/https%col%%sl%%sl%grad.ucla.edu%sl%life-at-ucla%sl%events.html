Events Calendar

New Graduate Student Welcome

Welcome our newest graduate Bruins into the UCLA community. Hosted annually in September/October.

Past events:

2020 | 2019 | 2018 | 2017 | 2016 | 2015

Postdoctoral Scholars & Mentors Ceremony

Postdoctoral scholars and their mentors are honored for their important contributions to the university’s interrelated missions of research, teaching, and service. Hosted annually in April.

Past events:

2019 | 2018 | 2017 | 2015

UCLA Grad Slam

All master’s and doctoral students are challenged to visually present their graduate research in under 3 minutes using up to 3 slides with the help of coaching sessions and workshops. Hosted annually in March/April.

Past events:

2021 | 2020 | 2019 | 2018 | 2017 | 2016 | 2015

UCLA Doctoral Hooding Ceremony

Celebrate the achievements of doctoral graduates with mentors, peers, family, and friends. Hosted annually in June.

Past events:

2021 | 2020 | 2019 | 2018 | 2017 | 2016 | 2015
