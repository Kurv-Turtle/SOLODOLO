Performance

  • Hugo Armstrong (BFA 98), stage and screen actor (Waiting for Godot, Bleed Rail, Land of the Tigers) — Ovation Award
  • Matt August (MFA 97), director (How the Grinch Stole Christmas, Discord: The Gospel According to Thomas Jefferson, King Charles III)

  • Michael Cudlitz (BFA 90), screen actor (The Walking Dead, The Kids Are Alright, Southland, Band of Brothers) — Critics Choice Award

  • Nataki Garrett (MFA 02), director, artistic director of Oregon Shakespeare Festival

  • Michael Jung (MFA 94), director, VP of Theatrical Development, Walt Disney Imagineering
  • Jessica Kubzansky (MFA 94), director, co-artistic director of Boston Court Pasadena
  • Debi Manwiller (BFA 83), casting director (24, Chicago Hope, The Blacklist, Rudy) — 2 Primetime Emmy Awards
  • Terence McFarland (MFA 03), producer, Associate Executive Director, The Younes and Soraya Center for the Performing Arts at CSUN; former CEO, LA Stage Alliance
  • Katherine Noon (MFA 90), director, co-founder and artistic director of The Ghost Road Company
  • Poor Dog Group, experimental theater collective
  • Condola Rashad (BFA 08), stage/screen actor and musician (Billions, Romeo and Juliet, The Trip to Bountiful, Stick Fly, Ruined, Steel Magnolias) — 2 Tony Award nominations
  • Paul Reubens (73), screen actor, creator of Pee-wee Herman and Pee-wee’s Playhouse — 3 Primetime Emmy nominations
  • Amy Keating Rogers (MFA 94), story animation writer (The Powerpuff Girls, My Little Pony: Friendship is Magic, Johnny Bravo, Dexter’s Laboratory) — 4 Primetime Emmy nominations, 2 Daytime Emmy nominations
  • Douglas Rushkoff (MFA 86), media theorist, author (Team Human, Present Shock: When Everything Happens Now), documentarian
  • Katey Sagal (72), screen and voice actor (Sons of Anarchy, Married with Children, Futurama), singer-songwriter — Golden Globe Award
  • Catherine Sullivan (BFA 92), artist, director and filmmaker — Alpert Award in the Arts
  • Cecily Strong (BFA 06), stage and screen actor, comedian (Saturday Night Live)
  • Bresha Webb (BFA 07), screen actor (A Fall From Grace, Meet the Blacks, Ride Along 2, Marlon, Grey’s Anatomy)
  • Derek Webster (BFA 93), screen actor (NCIS: New Orleans, Ray Donovan, The Whispers)
  • Michole Briana White (91), stage and screen actor (Jitney, Bulrusher, Stick Fly, 100 Centre Street) — Obie, Drama Desk, and Ovation Awards
  • Chi-wang Yang (MFA 07), director, digital media artist, co-founder of Cloud Eye Control
