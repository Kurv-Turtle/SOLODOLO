The Career Center is constantly adding new events and workshops. Discover which is right for you.

  • Employer Information Sessions

Featured Events

Career Week

Spring Quarter

Career Week is a series of workshops and events held annually. Students are encouraged to explore different career opportunities and connect with industry professionals.

Master’s & PhD Conference

Biennial

UCLA’s Master’s & PhD Career Development Conference connects more than 100 advanced degree alumni to workshops and breakout sessions that focus on the intersection of career, identity and the future.

Hire UCLA

Fall and Winter Quarters

More than one hundred employers attend this fair each quarter. Opportunities range from business to government and everything in between.

Engineering & Technical Fair

Fall and Winter Quarters

Our largest tech event, the Engineering & Technical Fair gives students the opportunity to network directly with engineering & tech professionals. Past employers include Amazon, Hulu, eBay and Intel.

 

Admit UCLA

Fall Quarter

Held each Fall, Admit UCLA attracts a mix of graduate and professional schools reps who can help answer all of your questions about post graduate education.

Tech Summit

Spring Quarter

Tech Summit is an interactive and informative mini-conference that discusses how tech is influencing all industries and why diversity is important in the Tech Space.

 

Inaugural Future of Work Conference: Navigating Disruption (ARCHIVE)

The UCLA Career Center's first-ever conference in May 2021 was a great success! View the program details and videos here: https://career.ucla.edu/events/future-of-work
