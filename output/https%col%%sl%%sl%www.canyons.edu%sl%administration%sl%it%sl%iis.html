Infrastructure and Information Security

Infrastructure and Information Security is responsible for operating and maintaining the District's system infrastructure and website services.

 

Canyons M App

The Canyons M app allows convenient access to the university's academic resources, information, and gives you the ability to personalize  information on your phone or mobile device.

      Sign into the app using your Canyons ID login. If you cannot get in, please follow the instructions below:

                Canyons ID Login for Students: https://www.canyons.edu/administration/it/resources/canyonsid-student.php

                                                            Canyons ID Login for Staff: https://www.canyons.edu/administration/it/resources/portalguard.php


Download the official Canyons M app for free in your mobile devices app store.

 Available for download:

Apple App Store

Google Play Store

If you do not have access to a smart device, you can login using your PC at m.canyons.edu.
