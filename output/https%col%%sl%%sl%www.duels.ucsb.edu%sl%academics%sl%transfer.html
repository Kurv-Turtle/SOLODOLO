Transfer Student Center

The Transfer Student Center, located in the UCSB Library, is a one-stop shop for transfer students looking to meet other transfer students, attend workshops, and find out about many other resources designed to support transfer student success.  Transfer students seeking L&S College Academic Advising can also go to the Transfer Student Center to meet with one of the two full-time advisors on staff.
