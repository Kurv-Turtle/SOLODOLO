The University of California, Riverside has partnered with ComPsych®, the Faculty Staff Assistance Program provider. ComPsych offers GuidanceResources® services whose benefits provides UCR faculty, staff and their household family members confidential support, resources and information for personal and work-life issues. View the ComPsych program flyer or the ComPsych brochure.

The Tools You Need Today

There can be added stress and anxiety around the holidays. Read the ComPysch Newsline to learn about helpful tools and resources that your employees can utilize all season long.

The following services services are provided at no charge.

GuidanceResources® Benefits

Confidential Counseling

GuidanceResources® benefits are designed to provide free short-term counseling services for UCR faculty, staff and their household family members to help handle concerns constructively, before they become major issues. Call anytime about concerns such as marital, relationship and family problems; stress, anxiety and depression; grief and loss, job pressures and substance abuse.

Work Life Solutions

The work-life specialists at ComPsych will do the research and provide qualified referrals and customized resources for child and elder care, moving, pet care, college planning, home repair, buying a car, planning an event, selling a house and more. For detailed information about the services provided, please review the Work-Life Benefits flyer.

Financial Information

With the GuidanceResources® benefit, you can get answers about budgeting, debt management, tax issues and other money concerns from on-staff CPAs, Certified Financial Planners® and other financial experts, simply by calling your toll-free number. To get additional information, please review the Financial Benefits flyer.

Legal Support

With the GuidanceResources® program, there is an attorney “on call” whenever there are questions about legal matters. Speak with on-staff licensed attorneys about legal concerns such as divorce, custody, adoption, real estate, debt and bankruptcy, landlord/tenant issues, civil and criminal actions and more. If representation is required, you can be referred to a qualified attorney for a free 30-minute consultation and a 25 percent reduction in customary legal fees. Learn more about legal support by reviewing the Legal Benefits flyer.

GuidanceResources® Online

Visit GuidanceResources® Online to access timely, expert information on thousands of topics, including relationships, work, school, children, wellness, legal, financial and free time. You can search for qualified child and elder care, attorneys and financial planners, as well as ask questions, take self-assessments and more.

Bright Horizons Care Advantage

To help you balance work and family responsibilities, UC offers Bright Horizons Care Advantage, a comprehensive web-based resource.

The Bright Horizons Care Advantage programs help you find pre-screened care resources that fit your family’s needs including the Extended Family Support Program. For more information about this system wide UC benefit, visit the UC Family Care Resources webpage or review the Bright Horizons Enhanced Family Supports FAQs.
