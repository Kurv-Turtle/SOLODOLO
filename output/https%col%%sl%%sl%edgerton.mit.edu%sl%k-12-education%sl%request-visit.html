Request a Visit

When a request is submitted, you will receive an e-mail copy of your submission, but the request is not a final confirmation. A separate confirmation e-mail will be sent once all plans are agreed upon.

We are able to book one lesson for one group per day during public school hours; simultaneous sessions are not possible.

  • Note that our on-line lessons are available to groups ONLY; there is no single child sign-up. (Homeschooling groups are expected to arrange for the appropriate number of students within their organizations; the Edgerton Center will not register individual families.)

Not all lessons are available as Virtual sessions; at this time, only Flashlight Building and Quizboards are available as Remote sessions.
