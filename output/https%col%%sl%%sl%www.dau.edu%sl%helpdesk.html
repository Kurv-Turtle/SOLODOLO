New Accounts

Are you a new student or need a DAU.edu account? New students and those needing accounts to access DAU.edu resources must submit the DAU SAAR so an account can be created. After receiving your login credentials, you will have access to register for courses in the Virtual Campus or login to the DAU.edu website.
