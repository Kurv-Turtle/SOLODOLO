Friends of CalArts

CalArts Art School Practicum course titled Waste Not in which students repurpose waste materials for art making.

Friends of CalArts (FoCA) are a collective of alumnx, families, and friends of the Institution who are dedicated to supporting our mission through annual gifts. 

 

Founded in 1971, the group’s ongoing generosity has helped the Institute award scholarships and provides many young artists with the resources and tools necessary to achieve their creative potential. 

Throughout the year, special programs and events are planned for FoCA donors that highlight our ongoing educational endeavor grounded in openness, experimentation, critical engagement, and creative freedom.


Membership Levels and Benefits

Gifts totaling $100+ / year for CalArts Recent Alumnx (1-5 years out)
Gifts totaling $1,000+ / year for CalArts Alumnx (6+ years out), parents and family members, and friends of the Institute

All membership levels receive:

  • Quarterly Friends of CalArts spotlight communication
  • Limited edition CalArts poster designed by a CalArts graphic designer
  • ​Discounts at REDCAT
  • Invitations to unique events
  • ​Behind the scenes tour of CalArts (even if virtual!)

Make a gift now to join Friends of CalArts

Monthly payments are welcome!
