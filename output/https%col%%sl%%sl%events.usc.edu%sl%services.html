SERVICES WE OFFER

The Office of Cultural Relations and University Events offers many services that are available to your department, organization or school. Although most of our staff work directly with schools to help them achieve their goals in event management and presentation, we also offer auxiliary support services to faciliate your event production.

Some services include staffing check-in tables with students, printing menu cards and dining place cards or helping with your RSVP process by creating an online rsvp component for your event.

Event Registration Services

The Office of Cultural Relations and University Events provides web page creation services to allow guests to place reservations or even purchase tickets.

Event Registration System

The Office of Cultural Relations and University Events utilizes an electronic reservation system to aid in reservations and ticket purchasing. This ESVP, or online RSVP and event information system, allows guests to make their reservations online. Event planners have constant access to their registered guests information.

We offer a number of different services in regards to our ESVP system:

Basic ESVP - consists of the creation of the ESVP with no phone or response card support.
*Please allow two business days for pages to be posted

ESVP with phone support - consists of the creation of the ESVP and utilizing the Cultural Relations and Events Reservation Center, which is staffed from 8:30am - 5pm Monday through Friday, to process phone reservations.
*Please allow two business days for pages to be posted

ESVP with credit card transactions - consists of the ESVP with phone support and processing of events that have credit card transactions. Please note the cost difference for events with over 500 credit card transactions.
*Please allow five business days for pages to be posted

ESVP with international credit card transactions - consists of the ESVP with phone support and processing of events that have credit card transactions. Please note the cost difference for events with over 500 credit card transactions.
*Please allow five business days for pages to be posted

*Customizations of ESVP pages may require additional time. Please contact the Reservations Office at rsvp@usc.edu for further details
