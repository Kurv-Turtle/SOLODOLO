PROCEEDING SAFELY

FACE COVERING UPDATE

Based upon recent changes to the COVID-19 guidance issued by the CDC and the California Department of Public Health (CDPH), the University has updated its requirements for wearing a face covering in indoor public settings.
Based on this new guidance as well as current COVID-19 community levels within the region, as of April 1, 2022, the University will no longer require wearing face coverings indoors.

The University values the personal choice of wearing a face covering and suggests that members of our community carefully consider the recommendations of the CDC and CDPH. Those who wish to continue to wear face coverings regardless of vaccination status are free to do so without fear of retaliation.

Parking Information

Park in the   Nutwood Parking Structure   for all performances, Visual Arts exhibitions and other non-performance activities. Enter at Arts Drive or Nutwood Avenue.

Parking is free Friday evenings (after 5 pm) and all day on Saturday and Sunday.

At all other times, daily permits are required and can be purchased for $10 on the 2nd and 4th floors of the Nutwood Parking Structure. Cash is not accepted. Visa, MasterCard and Discover only.
