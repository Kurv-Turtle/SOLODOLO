How do I download a SCORM package from my Course in TITANium?

You will learn how to download a zip SCORM package from your course in Titanium.

1. Log on to Titanium by clicking the Titanium icon from your University Portal.

2. Click the Semester icon containing the course.

3. Launch the course you want to download SCORM package from.

4. Click the Gear icon.

5. Click Turn editing on.

6. Scroll to the Course section that contain your SCORM package.

7. Click Edit.

8. Click Editing settings.

9. Click on the SCORM package.

9.1. Click Download to download your SCORM package and save the zip file to your computer.
