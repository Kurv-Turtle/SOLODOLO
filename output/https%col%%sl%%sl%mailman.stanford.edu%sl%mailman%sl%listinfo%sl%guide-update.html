guide-update -- Announces updates to the Administrative Guide

 

About guide-update
English (USA)

An email is sent to those on this list announcing updates to policies in the
Administrative Guide. For further information
contact guide-update-owner@lists.stanford.edu

To see the collection of prior postings to the list, visit the guide-update Archives. (The current archive is only available to the list members.)

Using guide-update
To post a message to all the list members, send email to guide-update@lists.stanford.edu.

You can subscribe to the list, or change your existing subscription, in the sections below.

Subscribing to guide-update

Subscribe to guide-update by filling out the following form. You will be sent email requesting confirmation, to prevent others from gratuitously subscribing you. This is a private list, which means that the list of members is not available to non-members.
