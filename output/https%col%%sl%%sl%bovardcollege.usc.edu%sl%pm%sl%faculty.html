Taught by Expert Professionals

Our student-centric program was built through partnerships with acknowledged experts in this exciting field, who understand what it takes to become a leader in project management. They are eager to share their insights and experience in all sectors — from technology and finance to transportation and other areas spanning the public and private realms. They are also prepared to introduce you to areas of specialization within project management, such as risk management or schedule management.

In pursuing your online Master of Science in Project Management, you will gain:

  • Mentorship from thought leaders and experienced professionals in all realms of project management
  • Training at the cutting edge of the field
  • Individualized tools and guidance to aid you in accomplishing your professional objectives
  • A technologically advanced online learning environment specifically designed to support the needs of busy working professionals

Bolstering the MS in Project Management program is an advisory committee of preeminent experts who ensure that our curriculum’s design stays current in instilling the knowledge and skills most sought after by employers.

Advisory committee members have decades of collective experience at Fortune 500 companies, government agencies, and non-profit organizations across the public and private sectors. Their expertise encompasses manufacturing, transportation, technology, banking, hospitality and other industries. Adept in the Agile and Six Sigma approaches to project management, they share these and other methodologies to keep the program at the forefront of all aspects of this growing field, including portfolio management, supply chain management and inventory management.
