Zipcar

Fast. Convenient. Affordable. Environmentally Friendly.

With Zipcar on campus, it just got easier to live without a car.

Need a car? Borrow a Zipcar! USC has partnered with Zipcar to bring self-service, on-demand car sharing to the area. To use Zipcars, simply register as a member, reserve a car online or by phone, use your Zipcard to enter the car, and drive away. Return the car to the same location where you picked it up.

As a member, you get:

Access to Zipcars 24/7.

Discounted hourly rates are available for USC faculty, staff, and students age 18 and older.

Gas, insurance, and maintenance are included for free.
