Electric Vehicle Charging

Breadcrumb
  •  
  • Electric Vehicle Charging

Transportation and Parking Services supports electric vehicle users by providing electric vehicle charging options to encourage greener and cleaner transportation. When on campus, users can choose from Level I and Level II charging options.

Level I Charging
(120 volt/20 amps)
Faculty/Staff/Students Only

Level I charging is the newest charging option for faculty, staff and students and is expected to continue to expand as a result of the lower installation costs and user fees. Electric vehicle users who wish to use Level I charging spaces in commuter lots must purchase an Electric Vehicle Level I Charging permit (see rates) in addition to their parking permit. There are no access cards, phone apps or special processes to use these spaces once you are registered with Transportation and Parking Services.

To use a level I charging space, users will need to provide their own charging cord. Cords will need to have a standard residential 20 amp wall plug.

Service Locations:

  • Big Springs 2 parking structure - 93 ports
  • Big Springs 1 parking structure (Residence Hall/Campus Apartment permit holders only) - 8 ports
  • Lot 6 - coming in 2022

Level II Charging
(240 volt/30 amps)
Faculty/Staff/Students/General Public

Level II charging on the UCR campus is available to the campus community as well as the general public and is provided through the Chargepoint network. Users must have a valid UCR parking permit and initiate a charging session through Chargepoint to utilize these charging spaces. To use the Chargepoint network, users must have a current account with Chargepoint. Current rates for the use of the Level II Chargepoint chargers are posted on the charger screen. Fees are collected for the total amount of time the charger/space is occupied.

Service Locations:

  • Lot 1 - 4 ports (1 port requires ADA parking permit)
  • Lot 6 - 4 ports
  • Lot 9 - 4 ports
  • Lot 15 - 4 ports
  • Lot 20 - 2 ports
  • Lot 22 - 8 ports (not eligible for UCR discount)
  • Lot 24 - 12 ports
  • Lot 27 - 8 ports (not eligible for UCR discount)
  • Lot 30 - 2 ports
  • Lot 50 - 4 ports
  • Big Springs 2 parking structure - 12 ports

Please view the Chargepoint Electric Vehicle Charging Station Map  for location information.

Other Electric Vehicle Services

Level III charging is available at the Center for Environmental Research and Technology facility located at 1084 Columbia Avenue, Riverside, CA 92507.

UC Intellicenter, home of UC Path, has Chargepoint Level II chargers available in the employee parking lot. These chargers are operated by UCOP and current rates are listed on the chargers.

UCR Palm Desert has Chargepoint Level II chargers located in Lot B. These chargers are operated by the UCR Palm Desert campus and current rates are listed on the chargers.

Chargepoint (Level II Charging) Account Directions

  • STEP 1: CREATE A CHARGEPOINT ACCOUNT

    To use ChargePoint EV charging stations, users must first sign up for a ChargePoint account online or by calling ChargePoint Customer Service at 888.758.4389.

    1. Creating a ChargePoint account:

          a. Please visit www.chargepoint.com to create an account.

          b. Download the ChargePoint App or request a ChargePoint card that can be mailed to the address provided during registration.

  • STEP 2: REGISTER FOR YOUR UCR CONNECTION

    UCR Faculty, Staff and Students will receive a $1 per hour discount by adding the connection code UCREV1 to their ChargePoint account.  Once you have logged in to ChargePoint's system, click on the title "Connections".  At the top of the Connections screen, you will see a place to enter the Connection code.  Enter the Connection code and right click to apply.  You will be asked for your UCR employee ID or student ID number.  After you have submitted your application, Transportation Services will be notified and asked to approve the application before the discount is applied.  Requests are usually processed the next business day.

    NOTE: Lot 22 and Lot 27 chargers are not eligible for the UCR discount.

    As a reminder, ChargePoint will bill you for your usage based on the length of time your vehicle is connected to the charging station so please make sure you move your vehicle accordingly.

     
  • STEP 3: HOW TO START YOUR CHARGING SESSION

    Use the following steps to charge your vehicle at the ChargePoint charging stations on campus.

    1. At the charging station
      1. Tap to charge. With the ChargePoint app installed, open the app and hold your phone over the reader symbol on the station. Your payment will be received via credit card or Android/Apple Pay.
        • iPhone: In the ChargePoint app, go to Menu, select Account, and then Manage Cards. Pick "set it up," add your ChargePoint pass to Apple Wallet and tap your phone on the station to charge.
        • Android: Enable NFC, turn your phone screen on and tap your phone on the station to charge.
        • Note: To use tap to charge, you'll need iPhone SE, 6, 6 Plus, 6S, 6S Plus, 7 and 7 Plus running iOS 10 or newer, Apple Watch Series 2, Series 1, and 1st generation or Android 4.4 or later with NFC.
      2. Use the ChargePoint card. Tap your activated ChargePoint card on the card reader symbol on the station.
    2. Contact ChargePoint Customer Service. Call 888.758.4389 to start a charging session over the phone.
      • The charging port will be released.
      • Plug the port in to your vehicle to begin charging.
      • The ChargePoint app can now provide you with updates about your vehicle's charging status and notifications when your vehicle is fully charged.
    3. Begin charging session
    4. End charging session
      • Disengage the port from your vehicle and place it back on the ChargePoint station to end your session.
