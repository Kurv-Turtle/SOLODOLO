Open Recreation

Open recreation (or open rec) provides our members and their guests with the opportunity to participate in non-structured activities at the UCR Student Recreation Center. Multi-purpose rooms, courts, and spaces are available on a first come, first serve basis. Open Recreation is turned over every thirty minutes as needed, if you require assistance please see the front counter.

Please visit our Modified Hours and Closures prior to visiting the SRC, as these hours are subject to change. Also, visit our SRC Reservation Calendar, for an in-depth schedule of SRC room/location closures and reservations. 

NOTE: Open recreation in Multi-purpose rooms and racquetball courts are turned over on the hour and are subject to required closures. Please make sure to check R'Rec screens for most up-to-date availability.
