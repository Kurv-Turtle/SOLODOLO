Exhibit: Uprooted — The Incarceration of Japanese Americans

February 17 – June 30, 2022 every Monday, Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday & Friday with exceptions | 10 a.m.-4 p.m. | Bancroft Library
This exhibition marks the 80th anniversary of Executive Order 9066, mandating the forced removal of Japanese Americans from their homes on the West Coast and into incarceration camps inland for the duration of World War II. This exhibition tells some of the stories of that traumatic time through official documents and the response of Japanese Americans.MORE about Uprooted — The Incarceration of Japanese Americans
