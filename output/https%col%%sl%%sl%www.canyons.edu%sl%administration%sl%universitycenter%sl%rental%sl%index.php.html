Rental Information

Situated on the southwest corner of the College of the Canyons property overlooking Interstate 5, the 110,000-square-foot center features 23 "smart" classrooms,  two computer laboratories, six meeting/seminar rooms, a lecture hall/theater and a large outdoor patio. Many rooms offer sweeping views of the Santa Clarita Valley and the various mountains that define the boundaries of the valley and offers a variety of room rental options. Rooms are available to rent on an hourly basis, with a two-hour minimum.

Technical support and food service are available. In addition, wireless and video teleconferencing capabilities exist throughout the building. The campus is convenient to two major freeways -- Interstate 5 (Golden State Freeway) and State Route 14 (Antelope Valley Freeway) -- and is easily accessed by buses operated by Santa Clarita Transit.
