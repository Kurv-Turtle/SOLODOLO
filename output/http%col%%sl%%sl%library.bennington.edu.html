Juneteenth

In recognition of Juneteenth, the library will have slightly altered hours the following day, Monday, June 20th. On Monday, 6/20, the library will open at 12pm and close at the usually scheduled 11:30pm. We will resume our normal hours, 9am - 11:30pm, on Tuesday, 6/21.

Summer Hours

Crossett will be open during the summer:

  • Monday - Thursday, 9am - 11:30pm
  • Friday, 9am - 11pm
  • Saturday and Sunday, 12pm - 11pm

The 24-hour room is available when Crossett Library is closed.
