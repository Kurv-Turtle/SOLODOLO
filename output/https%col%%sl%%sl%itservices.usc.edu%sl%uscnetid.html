USC NetID Account Services

Last modified: January 10, 2022

  • Update your secondary email This email is used to confirm changes to your account

  • Forgot your password Create a new password if you’ve forgotten
  • Manage your 2FA (DUO) devices Add or remove your devices in the Duo Security two-factor authentication service
  • Enroll in 2FA DUO An extra layer of security is added when you access USC resources

Your USC NetID is a username that signs you in to central USC online tools and resources, including the wireless network, MyUSC, Blackboard, Google Apps, Microsoft Office 365, Trojan Time, and Workday.

All USC faculty, staff, and students receive a USC NetID account, also known as your Shibboleth or enterprise account. Some USC schools and departments maintain local systems that are not dependent on the USC NetID account.
