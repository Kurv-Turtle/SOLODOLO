Graduate Admission

Princeton prepares graduate students for distinguished careers in research and teaching, and as leaders in the public and private sectors.

Nan Xue's Story
.

A graduate student in mechanical and aerospace engineering, Nan Xue studies complex fluids, such as how layered café lattes can provide insights into the engineering and manufacture of layered materials.
LEARN MORE

Sajda Ouachtouki's Story
.

Sajda Ouachtouki, a master's student in public affairs, has studied and worked on women's rights during her time at Princeton, from launching an anti-domestic violence co-creation program in Morocco to developing a gender strategy for Tunisia and Rwanda.
