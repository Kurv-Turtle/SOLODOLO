The Frances C. Arrillaga Alumni Center

The Arrillaga Alumni Center is your home away from home on the Stanford campus. Conveniently located on the corner of Campus and Galvez, your alumni center is a space for you to enjoy during your time back on the Farm.

From office services to convenient dining, the Frances C. Arrillaga Alumni Center has your on-campus needs covered.
