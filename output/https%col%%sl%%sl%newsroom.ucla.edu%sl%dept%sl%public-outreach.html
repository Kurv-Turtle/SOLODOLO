What is Public Outreach?

UCLA is committed to convening important conversations on complex issues. We promote the free exchange of ideas on and off campus to engage Angelenos, Californians and those across the country and globe. We partner across campus and with organizations and media to bring smart discussions to venues in and outside of Westwood.
