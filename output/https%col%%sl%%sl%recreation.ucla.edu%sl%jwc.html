John Wooden Center

The John Wooden Center is available to registered UCLA students, faculty, staff, and retirees. Students can use your bruin card for access. Faculty, staff and retirees, please register for a Rec membership.

University symptom monitoring survey required for access. Face covering required in all indoor spaces at all times.

Updated Policies and Procedures

COVID-19 Safety

  • Must present a current certificate of clearance from the UCLA Symptom Monitoring Survey for facility access and/or class participation.
  • Anyone displaying symptoms of illness may be asked to leave the facility and/or class.
  • Face coverings are required in all spaces at all times.

Facility Policies

  • Only UCLA Recreation Personal Fitness Trainers may conduct training sessions in any UCLA Recreation facility.

  • You must be at least 16 years of age to use strength areas, unless otherwise determined by instructor-led activity or program.

  • Courtyard will be closed in inclement weather, unsafe conditions and for scheduled classes.

For assistance:

  1. Report any blood, injury, equipment malfunction or facility feedback to the FITWELL staff immediately.

  2. For help or questions, including guidance and lifting instruction, see FITWELL staff.

  3. For special accommodations, see FITWELL staff.

Participant Policies

  • A top, bottoms and shoes are required, unless otherwise determined by instructor-led activity or program.

  • Closed toed/closed heel athletic shoes are required.

  • Clothing with buttons, zippers and studs is prohibited on upholstered equipment or any cardio seat.

  • Clothing or towel must provide a barrier between skin (except hands), upholstery and mats.

  • Towels are highly recommended.

  • Backpacks and bags are not allowed on the floor, except in the Cardio Zones. Personal belongings should be secured in a locker.

  • Do not hang any item on cardio equipment.

  • Beverages must be in a spill-proof, unbreakable, re-sealable container. No spitting.

  • Maintain personal hygiene as to not be disruptive to the environment.

  • No strongly scented deodorants, perfumes or colognes.

  • Be respectful of all equipment, patrons and staff.

  • Follow posted zone-specific policies and all staff directions.

Equipment:

  • Dumbbells, plates, and bars should not be dropped from any height.

  • Collars must be used on all bars when weight is added.

  • Do not lean bars or throw balls against walls, windows or ceilings. No contact with windows during any activity.

  • Do not stand bars or dumbbells vertically on floor.

  • Rerack dumbbells, barbells, plates and all other equipment to designated storage locations after each use.

  • Spotters are recommended for all free weight exercises performed over the head, face, and chest.

  • Maintain a safe distance between users.

  • Equipment must be used as intended. Modifications are NOT allowed.

  • All free-standing movements must be performed in designated area.

  • Wipe off equipment after each use.

  • 30-minute time limit (including warm up and cool down) on all cardio equipment when others are waiting.

Access Restrictions

  • Only UCLA Recreation Personal Fitness Trainers may conduct training sessions in any UCLA Recreation facility.

  • You must be at least 16 years of age to use the cardio and strength areas, unless otherwise determined by instructor-led activity or program.

  • Courtyard will be closed in inclement weather, unsafe conditions and for scheduled classes.

Location

The John Wooden Center sits atop Parking Structure 4 on Westwood Plaza just south of Sunset Boulevard. It is in the heart of campus, at the intersection of Bruin Walk and Westwood Plaza, alongside the Intramural Field, Pauley Pavilion, the JD Morgan Center, Ackerman Union, and the Student Activities Center.
