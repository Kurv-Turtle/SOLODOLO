« Diversity, Equity, & Inclusion

The USC Career Center supports your diverse identities and experiences. Regardless of ability, visible or invisible, you deserve to find careers and organizations that honor your whole self. These resources provide guidance on disclosure, requesting accommodations, understanding your rights in the workplace, and highlight tools to help you find your ideal career.
