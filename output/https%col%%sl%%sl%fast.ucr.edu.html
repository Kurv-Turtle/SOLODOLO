The Financial and Administrative Services Team (F.A.S.T.) provides a range of services to campus clients, including financial, human resources, purchasing, planning, and scheduling support. A team of overachievers, F.A.S.T. constantly strives to provide optimized administrative services that are specifically targeted to enhancing our clients’ abilities to achieve their strategic goals. Core to this is respect for people, client engagement, and continuous improvement. 

If you need support from F.A.S.T., please submit a request via Workfront.
