Student Project Application

Last Updated:January 3, 2022
  • Student Handbook
  • Equipment
  • Equipment Cage
  • Student Project Application
< Back

The Student Project Application must be completed at least two (2) weeks prior to any type of production. Your request can be made for any dates in the current semester.

Equipment pickups and drop-offs will be scheduled by appointment only on Mondays-Fridays (except Holidays) during Cage hours.

All Cage equipment is due back by May 2nd, 2022. All Installation equipment is due back by May 6, 2022.

Students using their own equipment must submit an application for any projects related to the School of Film/Video.
