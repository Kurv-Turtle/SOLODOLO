University of Massachusetts Global BA Criminal Justice 


20212– 2023 Advising Information
College of the Canyons and University of Massachusetts Global

  • Criminal Justice is the system of practices and institutions of governments directed at upholding social control, deterring and mitigating crime, or sanctioning those who violate laws with criminal penalties and rehabilitation efforts.
  • The criminal justice field is vast and growing, providing rewarding opportunities to those who wish to prevent crime, protect the public, and promote justice. 

  •  

    Sheri Veges, Academic Advisor, sheri.veges@umassglobal.edu
    University of Massachusetts Global at College of the Canyons
    26455 Rockwell Canyon Rd.

  • Students and COC advisors may use the Course Articulation Database to verify articulation: http://services.brandman.edu/studentservices/TransferCredit/

  • Application

    • Complete an application at www.brandman.edu (Select “Santa Clarita Campus”)
    • Application is completely free and available online
    • No application deadline, but it is highly recommended that students apply early in order to receive financial aid. 

    GPA and minimum credits

    • A minimum 2.0 GPA in transferable units
    • A minimum of 12 transferable credits

    Transcripts

    • Students need to submit all official transcripts from every college attended including AP test scores. Transcripts can be dropped off at the University of Massachusetts Global offices located in the University Center (204F or 204C) or mail transcripts to: University of Massachusetts Global, 16355 Laguna Canyon Rd., Irvine, CA 92618

    Cost

    • Undergraduate: $500/credit

              Military Undergraduate $250/credit

Bachelor of Arts in Criminal Justice

Term 2021-2022
Duration 1-2  years depending on transfer coursework
Costs Undergraduate Tuition: $500 per unit Technology Fee: $110 per session Application Fee: Free Graduation Fee: $150
Format Classes are offered as 8 week sessions
Scholarships College of the Canyons students receive 10% off tuition if they were enrolled at COC within the past year prior to enrollment at University of Massachusetts Global. Please complete the Scholarship Application.
