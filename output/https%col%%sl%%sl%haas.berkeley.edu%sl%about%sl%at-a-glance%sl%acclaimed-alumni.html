Acclaimed Alumni

Each year, Berkeley Haas recognizes alumni and business leaders who reflect our Defining Leadership Principles and excel in their respective fields. Whether their innovative thinking has disrupted an industry or their lifetime of achievements has advanced how we do business, Haas recognizes these leaders for their service and inspiration.

Business Leader of the Year/Lifetime Achievement Award

This Business Leader of the Year or Lifetime Achievement award is presented annually to one or more graduates or notable business leaders achieving prominence in their fields.

Awardees are selected for their career accomplishments, their dedication to and involvement with the school, and their stature as role models for alumni and students. These individuals have shown unfailing support and enthusiasm for the Haas School, including their involvement as advisors, speakers, and donors.

Cora Jane Flood Award

The Cora Jane Flood Award was established in 2007 and recognizes an alumna, friend or volunteer who has made a significant impact on the Haas School of Business through her philanthropy.

Flood provided the seed money for the College of Commerce on September 13, 1898 when she donated a Menlo Park residence and 550-acre tract of land to UC Berkeley.  This gift was the largest private contribution in the university’s then 30-year history and established the college now known as the Haas School of Business.
