All Facilities

UCLA Recreation manages a variety of the University's recreational and athletic facilities offering recreation, sport, and numerous multi-use options for student and university events. Visit individual facility pages for hours.

Facilities

Bike Shop

The UCLA Bike Shop is a Do-It-Yourself Workshop established as a partnership between UCLA Recreation and UCLA Transportation to encourage transportation by bicycle among current members of the UCLA Community.

 Bruin Fitness Center

BFIT is UCLA’s premier gym facility on The Hill that offers 60+ premium cardio machines and 50+ pieces of strength equipment.

 Challenge Course

The UCLA Challenge Course is an adventure-filled tool for team development, group bonding, and learning.

 Drake Stadium

Drake Track Stadium is a nine-lane 400-meter track with javelin, triple jump, long jump, high jump, and an infield events area.

Hitch Basketball Courts

Outdoor basketball court available for open recreation located on the Hill.

 Intramural Field

The Intramural Field accommodates Intramural Sports, Club Sports, Open Recreation, Summer Camps, and various special event activities.

 John Wooden Center

The John Wooden Center is in the heart of the campus and offers a multitude of courts, studios, and exercise machines for UCLA students and community members.

 Kinross Recreation Center

The Kinross Recreation Center offers an exclusive recreation environment for UCLA graduate students and faculty/staff.

 Los Angeles Tennis Center

The Los Angeles Tennis Center offers eight lighted tennis courts and the Straus Clubhouse.

 Marina Aquatic Center

The UCLA Marina Aquatic Center is UCLA’s premier water-front recreation facility just 10 miles from campus in scenic Marina Del Rey.

 Pauley Pavilion Presented by Wescom

Pauley Pavilion is regarded as one of the finest all-around collegiate facilities in the nation.

 Rental Center

Outdoor Adventure’s programming is designed to nurture and encourage a life-long love of outdoor pursuits.

 Rock Wall

People at all levels of experience are invited to climb at the Rock Wall on the first floor of the John Wooden Center.

 Student Activities Center

The Student Activities Center houses a gymnasium which hosts UCLA Athletics teams and serves as event space for Intramural Sports and student events. The Center also has an outdoor pool including lockers and shower facilities, and serves as home to multiple on-campus organizations for both undergraduate and graduate students.

 Sunset Canyon Recreation Center

A beautiful park like facility featuring outdoor lounge space, a community garden, pools, and sand volleyball courts.

Swimming Pools

UCLA Recreation manages six aquatic facilities throughout the campus and strives to meet the needs of all its participants by offering a variety of programs and services.

 Sycamore Park

A multipurpose open grass lawn adjacent to Sycamore Tennis Courts.

 Wallis Annenberg Stadium

The Wallis Annenberg Stadium accommodates Intramural Sports, Club Sports, Open Recreation, Summer Camps, and various special event activities.
