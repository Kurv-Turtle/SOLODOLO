Worldfest Education Abroad Fair 2022

Transform your professional future by learning abroad now. It can take up to a year to figure out what to study, where to go, and how to pay for it all, so begin your journey to career readiness with a week of events focused on everything related to education abroad. Our Worldfest Education Abroad fair runs from January 24th - 27th, and is open to all students, from UCR, the UC system, and outside it. Join them as you explore together how learning abroad can change your life.

 

 

When:

Monday, January 24 – Thursday, January 27, 2022, with times varying from 11:00 am to 5:00 pm

Where:  

Virtual - register for each session individually below. 

Questions?

Please contact the Education Abroad office educationabroad@ucr.edu

Semester at Sea Information Session
  • Hosted By:  Semester at Sea
  • Date/Time:  Tuesday, January 25, 11:00 a.m. – 12:00 p.m.
  • Register

Nearly 100 years ago, the idea for a floating university that would travel the world became the passionate pursuit of James Edwin Lough, a psychology professor at New York University. He believed changes needed to be made to traditional teaching methods of American universities and soon became a leader in a new educational movement. Travel and first-hand experience, he felt, must be part of every scholar’s education and he set out to find others who shared this vision. Join us as we introduce our study abroad program at sea.

We will raffle off $25 Barnes & Noble gift cards to undergraduate students who attend this session. Sponsored by the Highlander Abroad Fund.

Financial Aid & Scholarships - 
The Financial Aid Process & Education Abroad Information Session
  • Hosted By:  UC Riverside 
  • Date/Time:  Thursday, January 27, 12:00 p.m. – 1:00 p.m.
  • Register

Whether you plan to learn abroad for a summer, semester or year outside the U.S., you may be able to use most forms of federal, state, or institutional aid to support your study abroad costs (including virtual programs). The type of aid you can get - and the process you must follow - will depend on the program type. The key is getting started early! In this session, you will learn about FAFSA, types of financial aid, scholarships, and the UCR financial aid process from Sophia Dugbartey-Hodanu, a UCR Financial Aid Advisor for Education Abroad.
