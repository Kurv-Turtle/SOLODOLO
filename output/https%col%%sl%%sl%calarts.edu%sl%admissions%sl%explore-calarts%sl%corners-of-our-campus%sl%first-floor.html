First Floor

Numerous eclectic lab, production, and rehearsal spaces are located on the first floor, as well as several offices.

Acting Studio (F100)

Acting Studio (F100)

•School of Theater

A black box studio with a lighting rep plot, dimming capacity, and a mirrored wall. A general complement of studio furniture including acting cubes, rehearsal doors, and tables. In addition to curricular use as an acting studio, it also serves as one of our Directors Labs.

Animation Computer Labs

Animation Computer Labs

•School of Film/Video

Equipped with Apple Mac Pro workstations with Wacom Cintiq pen displays. A large format scanner is also available. Labs have 24-hour access during non-class times.

Learn More

Animation Downshooters

Animation Downshooters

•School of Film/Video

Downshooter stations comprised of DSLR cameras mounted on copystands with lights. Each station’s Mac computer is equipped with Dragonframe software for stop motion animation frame capture. The stations can be used for pencil testing or stop motion animation. An animation Lunchbox and a portable multi-plane are available.

Learn More

Art & Tech Studios

Art & Tech Studios

•

School of Art

Art and Technology MFA students share this common area, which functions as a working studio and a social “affinity space.” This studio allows for peer-to-peer communication and collaborative possibilities. Students are assigned a designated area within the common studio for their individual use.

Art School Maclab

Art School Maclab

•

School of Art •School of Dance •School of Theater

The MacLab facilitates the creation of digital and print-based work, motion graphics, and web design. The lab extends studio space and supports student use and the curriculum with a variety of software enabling editorial design, drawing, painting, photo manipulation, page layout, type design, UI/UX, 3-D rendering, motion graphics, and web design.

BFA Program in Art Studios

BFA Program in Art Studios

•

School of Art

Studio spaces for Program in Art BFA students. The studios are private and lockable, and are accessible 24/7 during the school year.

Center for Integrated Media

Center for Integrated Media

•

School of Art

The Center for Integrated Media supports all IM students across CalArts, enabling them to apply new technologies and integrate multiple media and disciplines into new forms of expression.

Learn More

Center for New Performance (inside Theater Office)

Center for New Performance (inside Theater Office)

•School of Theater

CalArts Center for New Performance (CNP) is the professional producing arm of CalArts, established to provide a unique artist- and project-driven framework for the development and realization of original theater, music, dance, media, and interdisciplinary projects. CNP extends the progressive work carried out at CalArts into a direct dialogue with professional communities at the local, national, and international levels.

Learn More

Costume Shop

Costume Shop

•School of Dance •School of Theater

At approximately 3,500 square feet of workspace, CalArts houses one of the largest educational costume shops in the country. Equipment includes, but is not limited to: a dye room, 12 cutting tables with shelving and tool cabinets underneath, 28 draping forms, eight industrial sewing machines, six domestic sewing machines, and six pattern file cabinets.

Learn More

Critical Studies Office

Critical Studies Office

•School of Critical Studies

The Critical Studies Office is the administrative hub of the entire School of Critical Studies. Here is where you can find faculty offices, the dean's office, and support staff.

Dance Digital Lab

Dance Digital Lab

•School of Dance

The Digital Lab has a projector and eight iMac computers, all installed with Logic Pro, Photoshop, and Final Cut Pro. This room is used by MFA students for web design classes. BFA students also use it for audio and music classes, where they learn multitrack mixing and other sound production techniques.

Dance Office

Dance Office

•School of Dance

The Dance Office is the administrative hub of the entire School of Dance. Here is where you can find faculty offices, the dean's office, and support staff.

Learn More

Gallery C113

Gallery C113

•School of Film/Video

This space is used for installation, performance, and multimedia work. It is a 588 square foot rectangle with an 80 square foot entrance area located inside the double entrance doors. There is a grid and acoustic treatments, and four 20-amp power strips mounted above the baseboard and below the grid on the long walls.

Learn More

Gamelan Room

Gamelan Room

•School of Music

Balinese and Javanese Music and Dance provide a comprehensive exploration. Players learn in small groups, working closely with world-renowned faculty and a full instrument collection, studying traditional and contemporary works. Students also a gain cultural understanding of the many roles gamelan music plays in Indonesian life.

Graduation Courtyard

Graduation Courtyard

Institute

CalArts graduation is a unique experience. The stage is designed by Theater students and the Gamelan Ensemble leads the procession of new graduates. This and much more takes place during this celebration of the arts at CalArts.

Learn More

Graphic Design Studios

Graphic Design Studios

•

School of Art

Graphic Design students are assigned individual desks and workspaces in a shared studio environment. You can check out student work and more at the Graphic Design blog at the link below.

Learn More

Live Action Computer Labs

Live Action Computer Labs

•School of Film/Video

A lab with iMac Pro workstations. Each workstation is equipped with hardware and software to meet live action production needs, including a full complement of A/V post-production software for digital editing, sound design, mixing, color grading, visual effects, delivery, and other tasks to assist students in the creation of their projects. Generally, during non-class times, labs are available except 4–7 am when there is no HVAC.

Mix Theater

Mix Theater

•School of Film/Video

The Mix Theater employs Pro Tools Ultimate system for audio editing and mixing. The space is ideal for monitoring in theatrical 5.1 surround sound with industry standard JBL cinema surround speakers and projected picture. An Avid S6 M40 control surface with 24 faders allows for audio editing, routing, mixing, and real-time automation.

Learn More

Permanent Set

Permanent Set

•School of Film/Video

This production set replicates the interior of a house complete with hardwood floor. The three main spaces are a living room, a kitchen, and a hallway. The set also includes a staircase landing, a bookcase, a fireplace, and an exterior window wall. There is a lighting grid and a DMX-capable lighting board.

Learn More

Photo/Media Studios

Photo/Media Studios

•

School of Art

Studio spaces for MFA students in the Program in Photography and Media. Each studio is private, individual, and lockable. Studios are accessible 24/7 during the school year.

Post Sound Suite and Sound Design Suite

Post Sound Suite and Sound Design Suite

•School of Film/Video

These 24-hour access post sound suites employ Pro Tools Ultimate software and allow recording via two mic/line inputs. A sound isolation booth is available. The spaces have an ITU 5.1 surround sound monitoring system for sound editing, design, and mixing.

Learn More

Print and Media Lab

Print and Media Lab

•

School of Art

The Print and Media Lab has various production methods available including screen printing, intaglio, lithography, letterpress, and bookbinding. The Lab also includes a Digital Lab consisting of seven workstations, two scanners, and basic small scale output for producing film for exposure as well as pre-production support for media projects.

Learn More

Sound Studio

Sound Studio

•School of Theater

A space for the development of content and programming for the sound design program.

Theater Office

Theater Office

•School of Theater

The Theater Office is the administrative hub of the entire School of Theater. Here is where you can find faculty offices, the dean's office, and support staff.

Learn More

Video Lab

Video Lab

•

School of Art

The Video Lab provides facilities, equipment, and technical instruction for making and exhibiting time-based artwork. It is a video-focused workspace with a wide selection of video and audio acquisition and exhibition tools available for checkout.

Video Studio

Video Studio

•School of Film/Video

This production space is used for classes, shoots, and installations and is 1,400 square feet with an adjacent control room. There is a lighting grid with movable and fixed lights. A fixed green screen curtain and flooring are available for digital compositing. There is also a slidable black curtain as well as production equipment.

Learn More

Videographics

Videographics

•School of Film/Video

A dedicated live analog video generation and manipulation facility, a distinctive resource and one of very few of its kind in the United States.
