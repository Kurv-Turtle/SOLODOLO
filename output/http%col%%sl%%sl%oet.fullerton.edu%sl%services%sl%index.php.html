OET Professional Services

OET has consulting, training, and production resources to help you create learning materials that can enhance students' acquisition and application of knowledge and skills. 
 
Our Instructional Designers (IDs) and Academic Technology Consultants (ATCs) are ready to meet faculty at any stage in their course development. Partner with OET's professionals to learn new teaching techniques and design a new online look for your face-to-face, online, or digitally enhanced courses. 
 
The IDs and ATCs at the Online Education and Training department can assist you in making your classes more engaging, interactive, and accessible.
 
Explore our services and visit our facilities to familiarize yourself with the resources available to faculty members.
