CAMPUS CONTACTS

To find out about the array of undergraduate research possibilities and how to proceed, please contact your departmental adviser, your faculty adviser, your professors, or use any of the following links. And as you begin to read through lists and talk with professors and staff members, think not only of your major field but of other areas as well. College of Letters and Science

The Undergraduate Research and Creative Activites office offers workshops, peer mentors, and even small grants to support your research.

College of Engineering

The Center for Science & Engineering Partnerships (CSEP) CSEP offers a range of programs from a two-week summer introduction to research for incoming freshmen, to early research exposure for lower and upper division students.

Ofi Aguirre, Director, Center for Science & Engineering Partnerships
805-893-7472
aguirre@cnsi.ucsb.edu

College of Creative Studies

The College of Creative Studies is small college for self-directed students within a world class research university, providing a rigorous, accelerated program facilitating original undergraduate work in 8 majors.

Office of Research

The Office of Research is responsible for this website, the Undergraduate Research brochure, and general information related to research. We can help identify the right people to contact to get started in undergraduate research.

Kelly Pillsbury, Research Development & Funding Opportunities Specialist
pillsbury@research.ucsb.edu
