REGISTRATION APPLICATION 

In order to be eligible to request funding through the Student Union, all student organizations must submit an application to be registered under the Student Union. 

Requirements: 

      • Attend the student organization fair 
      • Meet with the Assistant Director of Campus Life

 

Student Organizations Policies and Guidelines:

      • Membership must be open to all CalArts students.
      • All club and organization members must be currently enrolled at CalArts.
      • To remain in good standing, all Student Organizations must annually reregister during the academic school year.
      • Responsible for ensuring that all Student Organizations sponsored events abide by CalArts policies (as stated in the Student Handbook), Federal, State and Local laws.
      • Students shall not discriminate in practice against any person by reason of race, color, age, ethnic background, religion, or sexual orientation.
      • Student Organizations or any members thereof shall not conspire to engage in hazing or commit any act that injures, degrades, or disgraces, or tends to injure, degrade, or disgrace any fellow student or person attending CalArts.
