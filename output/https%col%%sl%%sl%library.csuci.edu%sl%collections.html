Digital Collections

ScholarWorks at CI

ScholarWorks is the shared open access, digital repository platform for the California State University System. Broome Library manages the campus instance and provides support for faculty, staff, and students to preserve and provide access to their work. The system is home to a wide variety of materials including journal articles, datasets, posters, capstone projects, theses, and much more. It documents the incredible research outputs of our community and demonstrates our commitment to equitable access to academic research.

University Archives

The University Archives collects and houses materials relating to campus development and local history from 1974 to the present. The collection includes artifacts, books, documents, memorabilia, newspaper articles, photographs, and videos centering on campus life at California State University, Northridge - Ventura Campus, California State University, Northridge – Channel Islands Campus, and California State University, Channel Islands. Portions of the University Archives have digitized, while the rest is available for research purposes by appointment.
