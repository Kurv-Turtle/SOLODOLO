  • Meetings are held in Room 137 of Hasley Hall at College of the Canyons, 26455 Rockwell Canyon Road, Santa Clarita, unless otherwise noted.

    Map & Directions | Campus Map

  • Business meetings begin at 4 p.m. with a closed session. Open sessions begin at 5 p.m. Times are subject to change, especially for special meetings, so please check the agenda.

  • The public is always welcome to attend open sessions. The public may not attend closed sessions, whose topics are limited to those set forth by state law.

  • Brown Act Changes

    Consistent with the amendments to the Brown Act made by Assembly Bill 361, members of the public wishing to provide public comment on closed session items, open session items, and those items not on the agenda that are within the jurisdiction of the Board of Trustees, may do so in real-time.

    If you have connected to the meeting using Zoom, please use the “Q&A” function. If you are calling into the meeting, please dial *9 to let staff know you wish to comment. Public comment will be accepted up until the board president calls for a motion on the board item being addressed.

    Zoom Instructions

    • Please use the Q&A function in Zoom

    • Indicate which item you wish to address or if you wish to make a general comment on a matter within the board’s subject-matter jurisdiction under “Items Not on the Agenda” sections at the beginning or end of the agenda.

    • Example: “I would like to request to speak on Item _____.” 

    Call-in Instructions

    • After calling into the meeting, please dial *9 to let staff know you wish to make a comment.

    Public Comment Process

    • The board president, with the assistance of district staff, will call on members of the audience who have requested to speak at the appropriate time; the moderator will allow you to speak by activating the function; and members of the audience will be able to make their comments. 

    • Board Policy 2350 limits each speaker to 3 minutes per subject and the total time per subject to 20 minutes. 

    • Board members are prohibited by the Brown Act from responding to comments made regarding topics not on the official agenda.

    • The district will no longer read emailed public comments aloud during the meeting. Emails sent before the meeting will be forwarded to the trustees for their review and consideration.

    • Submissions of public comments will be considered a public record under the Public Records Act and are therefore subject to public disclosure.

    Technical Issues

    • If you are having difficulty using the public comment protocols or procedures, please send an email to:

    boardmeetingcomments@canyons.edu

    • Individuals desiring to provide public comment through the use of an internet website, or other online platform, not under the district’s control, that requires registration to log in into a teleconference may be required to register by the third-party internet website or online platform to participate.

  • Meeting dates can change. When they do, a revised meeting calendar will be posted here and at the following locations at the Valencia campus:

    • Glass-enclosed announcement case on the north side of Seco Hall.
    • Glass-enclosed announcement case in the Student Center atrium.
    • Bulletin board near the reference desk of the Library.

  • The Board of Trustees will attempt to schedule joint meetings with other governing bodies such as the Associated Student Government, Wm. S. Hart Union High School District Governing Board, Santa Clarita City Council, and COC Foundation Board of Directors. These dates have been set aside so we can calendar joint meetings based on the availability of these organizations.

    * A single asterisk indicates the date is tentative and dependent on whether:

    • all board members are confirmed and able to attend and/or participate
    • outside expertise and/or presenters are available (if needed)

    ** Two asterisks indicates the date is tentative and awaiting confirmation by the Wm. S. Hart Union High School District Governing Board.
