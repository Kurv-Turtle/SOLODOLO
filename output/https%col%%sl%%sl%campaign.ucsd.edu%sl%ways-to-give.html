Ways To Give

Your gift of any size helps continue the nontradition at UC San Diego. Whether you are making a gift today that will have an immediate impact on the student experience, innovative research endeavors or our campus community, or are planning to leave a legacy gift for future Tritons, there are many ways to give to the Campaign for UC San Diego.

  • Online

    Visit UC San Diego’s convenient and secure online giving website today and donate by:

    • Credit card
    • Payroll deduction (for UC San Diego employees)
    • Setting up a recurring donation
    • Donate cryptocurrency
    Give Now

  • By Phone

    If you have questions about a gift or prefer to provide your credit card information by phone, please contact:

    UC San Diego Gift Processing
    (858) 534-5289

  • By Mail

    Please send your check made payable to
    “UC San Diego Foundation” to:

    UC San Diego Gift Processing
    9500 Gilman Drive #0940
    La Jolla, CA 92093-0940

    Note: checks for gifts to Regents funds should be made to “UC Regents”

Employer Gift Matching Program

Search for your employer in our matchfinder tool to learn if they will match your contribution. Many employers offer philanthropic matching gift programs that can double or even triple the impact of your donation.

Find Your Match

Planned Gifts

Explore how you can impact UC San Diego through a planned gift and the potential benefits for you and your loved ones. Contact the Office of Gift Planning at (858) 534-2249 or visit our website for information about:
– Gifts in your will, trust or eligible retirement plan
– Stock, real estate or appreciated assets
– Gifts that provide income

Securities and Wires

To learn more about the benefits of making a gift of appreciated securities, please contact us at (858) 534-4491 or (858) 534-2249 for more information. If you’d like to make a gift of appreciated securities or by wire, please click here.
