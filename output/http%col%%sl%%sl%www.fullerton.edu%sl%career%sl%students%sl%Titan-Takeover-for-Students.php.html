Titan Takeover Career Exploration Program Testimonial

 

 

Titan Takeover: Career Exploration Program

The Spring Hybrid version of the Job Shadow Program will take place during the month of April on an agreed upon date/time between participant and host. Titan Takeover will take place in hybrid format, meaning some hosts will be hosting in-person, others will be hosting virtually, and some are open to either format.

The purpose of the Titan Takeover: Career Education Program is to provide students with an opportunity to make a more informed decision regarding a career path that best fits their interests, passion, traits, and skills. The program will allow students to observe “what a day in the life of…” would look like in addition to receiving information and advice from career professionals.

 

TIMELINE

  • Application period: Thursday, January 27, 2022 - Friday, February 18th 2022
  • Students are notified of acceptance to Titan Takeover: Career Exploration Program During the week of March 1st

 How to Participate

  1. Check out the 2022 Titan Takeover Host List of employers participating in the Titan Takeover this April.
  2. Fill out the Hybrid Spring 2022  Titan Takeover Student Application.  
  3. Attend the Titan Take Over Orientation.

There will be multiple sessions for the orientation
All sessions will be IN-PERSON in the Career Center (LH 210G)
Students must participate in order to receive their assigned host's contact information.
More information will be provided upon acceptance to the program.

Hear it from the Students

“The Titan Takeover program was such a great learning experience! I was matched with several professionals in my same career path that I was able to network with. The shadowing experience has given me an opportunity to see a day in the life of a corporate office. It was insightful and inspiring, and I would definitely recommend it to anyone who is curious to see the real life work environment.”

“Titan Takeover was a valuable experience. Rather than learning in a classroom, you get to obtain real world experience from real professionals who were in the same position as you. It is very relatable and gives a much needed boost of confidence to see where you can be in a matter of a few years.”

“This experience opened my eyes to a possibility outside of what I thought I should do with my degree after I graduate, allowing me to understand that there are other possibilities out there that I should explore.”

“I had a great time doing the Titan Takeover: Job Shadow Program. This opportunity helped me solidify what career path I want to take and what I feel will be a good match for me and my skills. This was a great opportunity and I would recommend every student to do it.”

“Participating in Titan Takeover allowed me to view the workforce differently. Not only did I learn about the operations but I was able to understand their company culture. I also loved being able to apply what I learned in the classroom and having professional conversations with management.”
