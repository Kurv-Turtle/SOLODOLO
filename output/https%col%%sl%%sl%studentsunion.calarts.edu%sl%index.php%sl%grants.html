Why Grants?

We believe that every student should be able to have monetary support to help aid the work and art that they want to produce while at CalArts.

Students' Union Grants do just that. They are also reviewed by student representatives, who work hard to make sure all students are treated fairly.

For any questions or concerns please contact our Students' Union Treasurer:

treasurer@alum.calarts.edu
