Financial Accounting

  • Overview
  • Units & Staff
  • Policies & Guidance
  • Resources
  • Financial reports

We provide systemwide financial management, reporting, policy and procedures in support of UC's mission.

Financial accounting provides strategic leadership and support of systemwide financial functions including corporate accounting, federal and extramural costing policy, benefit plan accounting, endowment and investment accounting, payroll policy and compliance and taxation. We deliver efficient and effective financial services, provide best practices and guidance to our campus and medical center communities, develop and administer systemwide policies and procedures, and ensure the University’s financial systems are compliant with all University, State and Federal laws, rules and regulations.

Barbara Cevallos

Associate Vice President and Systemwide Controller
