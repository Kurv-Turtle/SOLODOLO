Search for research projects on a topic

Information on projects funded by UC IPM since 1979 is stored in this database. You may search for any combination of hosts, pests, disciplines, and principal investigators, for any range of funding year and by research program (Exotic Pests, UC IPM, or both). You'll be presented with details of all projects meeting the search criteria.

Specific variable search: Set one or more search criteria

To use, type the exact word or phrase (such as "cotton" under "host" or "aphid" under "pest") in the appropriate box(es). You may also click on any of the "list" links, then click on a name to select it. You'll be returned to this screen and your selection will automatically appear in the appropriate box.
