Student Health Insurance Office Contact

For further information and questions about student health insurance or billing, contact the Student Insurance Office. You may dial through the main USC Student Health line at 213-740-9355 (WELL) or call the office directly at 213-821-1550.

Office hours are Monday – Thursday 8:30 am – 5 pm / Fridays 9:30 am – 5 pm.
