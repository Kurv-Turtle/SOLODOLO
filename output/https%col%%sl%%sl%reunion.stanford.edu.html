Make Your Return to Stanford Unforgettable

Enjoy a unique selection of Reunion Homecoming events and programs. Learn from Stanford faculty on a variety of topics in Classes Without Quizzes. Explore today's campus with special tours and open houses. Dine under the stars at the elegant Dinner on the Quad. Reunite with friends (and meet new ones) at your Class Lunches, Parties, Panels, Mini-Reunions, Tailgates and community gatherings.

Reunion Homecoming details and Class Book instructions will be emailed to you over the next few months, but they won't reach you unless we have your current contact information.

Class Events

Intellectual Programs
