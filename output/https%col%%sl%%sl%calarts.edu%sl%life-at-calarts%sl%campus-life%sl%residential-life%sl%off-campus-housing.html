Off-Campus Housing

Living off-campus can be very rewarding and enjoyable, but deciding where to live can be stressful.

CalArts Residence Life has created this Off-Campus Housing Guide to assist students in finding housing in the Santa Clarita and greater Los Angeles areas. The collection of information is intended to assist and provide resources needed for living off-campus.
