Adobe Creative Cloud Pro

Adobe Creative Cloud Pro is a cloud-based subscription service utilizing your campus login in conjunction with the Adobe Creative Cloud Pro desktop and mobile apps to install and validate your subscription.

Each active CSUF faculty/staff member is entitled to one campus subscription for at-home use.

Note: Subscription expires annually. You will be notified via your campus portal to renew.

In order to receive a Creative Cloud Pro subscription, you will need to complete the two-step process beginning from your portal's "Featured Software for Faculty Staff and Students" section and by following the instructions on this page.  No CDs will be available for Adobe Creative Cloud Pro software.

 

ELIGIBILITY

You must be a CSUF faculty or staff member.
