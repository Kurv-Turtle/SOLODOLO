Welcome, Stanford students.

Undergrad or grad student? You’re in the right place. View the programs and resources that the Stanford Alumni Association (SAA) provides just for you by navigating to your class’s page from the menu above, or view the offers available to all students below.

TREEditions

Stanford traditions are everywhere—and we’ll tell you how to find them! We’ve created a poster of all the Stanford TREEditions you need to experience before you graduate; print one out at the link below.

Download TREEditions (PDF)

Student Alumni Facebook page

Be the first to hear about the newest student offerings from SAA, courtesy of your fellow students! The Student Alumni Facebook page is your source for upcoming event details, event photos and SAA employment and volunteer opportunities.

Visit Student Alumni on Facebook

Nerd Nation

SAA's got love for students, and one of the ways we show it every year is by showering you with the essential Nerd Nation freebies. We gifted a combined 1,200 t-shirts at our September 30 and November 18 giveaways this fall quarter. To get the scoop on fall 2022 dates, follow us on our socials.

Alumni Career Connections for Students

The Stanford alumni network is one of the most powerful tools for your job search and career exploration. Exclusively available to students and alumni, you can become part of the alumni community by creating a profile with SAA. You’ll receive an alumni.stanford.edu email and will have access to both the alumni directory and the private Stanford alumni LinkedIn group.

Learn more about career resources

Frances C. Arrillaga Alumni Center

The Frances C. Arrillaga Alumni Center isn’t just for alumni! Explore your future home away from home as you lounge by the giant fireplace, grab a cup of coffee at the café, study in the cozy library or relax outside in Ford Gardens.

See alumni center info

Reunion Crew

Stanford Reunion returned October 21-24, 2021 and SAA pulled off the biggest party on campus with the help of Stanford students like you. Check back next fall to join the interest list in order to sign up for shifts across Stanford Homecoming Reunion 2022.

Alumni Directory

Wherever you are, you can connect to your extended family of 230,000 Stanford alumni. Studying abroad, moving to a new city or getting familiar with an industry? Use the Alumni Directory to build your network and connect with alumni all over the world.
