Roxane Chenault

  • Operations Manager
  • Email: roxane@calarts.edu
  • Phone: 661-222-2778
  • Fax: 661-255-0938
  • Room: B323D

Roxane Chenault, Operations Manager in the Herb Alpert School of Music at CalArts, has worked within the School of Music since 1996.  She studied business and marketing at Los Angeles Valley College.  She works closely with our deans, faculty, staff and students.  One should see her for the following:
 
Appointments with the Deans
Academic issues
Space matters
Catalog / Class Schedule / Interim
Graduate Assistants
Security
 
Watch for her “Daily Announcement,” via your CalArts email account, she sends each morning to the entire School of Music community informing all of what’s happening.
