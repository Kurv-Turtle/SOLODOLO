Services

Last modified: April 9, 2021

This page lists all the items that USC faculty, staff, and students may request through ServiceNow, ITS’s customer service ticketing system. A USC NetID account is required to access these forms. If you do not see the service you wish to request in the lists below, please email consult@usc.edu.

If you need to report an issue with any of the services listed here, go to the Report an Issue page on the ITS Service Portal.

For full service pages for these services, see the Services menu at itservices.usc.edu.
