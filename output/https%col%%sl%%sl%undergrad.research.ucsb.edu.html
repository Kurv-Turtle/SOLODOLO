At UC Santa Barbara you have the opportunity to participate in cutting-edge research and creative opportunities. Participate in organized research programs, or jump in feet first and pursue your own research projects. Connect with faculty. Get published. Present your research in colloquia and conferences. Make a permanent and real impact in the fields of engineering, socials sciences, physical and life sciences, humanities and fine arts. Explore this site to discover what UCSB has to offer.
