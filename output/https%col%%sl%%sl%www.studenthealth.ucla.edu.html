The Arthur Ashe Student Health and Wellness Center (The Ashe Center) is devoted to providing quality, accessible, state-of-the-art healthcare and education to support the unique development of UCLA students. (Learn more about The Ashe Center)

    Planned Closures & Hours Updates

    Planned Closure from 1-3pm (6/15/22)

    The Ashe Center will be closed on Wednesday, June 15, from 1-3pm for a planned staff retreat. We apologize in advance for any inconvenience this may cause.

    Upcoming Holiday Closure (6/20/22)

    The Ashe Center will be closed on Monday, June 20, 2022, in observance of the Juneteeth university holiday. If you have COVID-19 symptoms such as fever and cough, receive a positive test result, or have had close contact with someone who tested positive please do the following: (1) Isolate yourself and stay at home; (2) Call The Ashe Center COVID-19 Hotline at (310) 206-6217 immediately.

    Please note: Processing of COVID self-tests via the campus vending machines may be affected by the university holiday.
