DAWIA Certification & Development Guides

While changes to DAWIA certification standards are normally implemented on October 1, of any given year, other aspects of the guide, in particular the Developmental Recommendations may be modified at any time. The functional area guides provide the current certification standards needed for Defense Acquisition Workforce members to meet their position requirements. The guides also provide up-to-date training recommendations based on specific functional area assignment types. Select your guide from the menu on the left or from the table below.
