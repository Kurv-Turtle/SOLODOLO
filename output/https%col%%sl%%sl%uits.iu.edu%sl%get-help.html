Common IT questions

The most frequently asked questions about IT at IU.
 

  • For your personal IU Username, use this Reset tool.
  • For Guest Accounts, use this Reset tool.
  • For Group or Affiliate Accounts, only the account owner or sponsor may reset the passphrase.
See also: Reset your IU passphrase

  • If your old Duo number is gone, or it is lost/broken, please call the Support Center.
  • If you have a new phone with the same phone number for Duo and Duo Push fails, add your new device to Duo by using the "Call/Text" options.
See also: Help for Two-Step Login (Duo)

  • Undergraduates generally have IU Gmail accounts.
  • Graduates, staff, and faculty use IU Exchange accounts.
  • Configure your email on clients: Gmail; Exchange.
See also: Accounts & email

  • If you're getting a "Stale Request" or other loading error, switch to Firefox or Chrome browsers and clear your browser’s cache.
  • Search Instructure Support for an array of topics: Students. Instructors.
See also: About Canvas at IU

  • Students, staff, and faculty should connect to eduroam. Having trouble? Use the Eduroam Network Setup Tool.
  • Guests on IU campuses should connect to the IU Guest Wi-Fi.
See also: Wi-Fi & Networks

  • Wireless devices must connect to IU PublicNet and be registered to function.
  • Wired devices must be registered. Based on your residence hall, you must use either commercial or IU-proprietary Ethernet cables.
See also: Register your gaming console or media device at IU

  • Students have two choices: Google at IU My Drive and Microsoft OneDrive at IU. Compare them.
  • Faculty and staff who work with institutional data have additional options.

  • Students, faculty, and staff can access IU-licensed software like Adobe and Microsoft at no extra cost.

CrimsonCard

CrimsonCard is the official photo ID card for all Indiana University campuses.

  • About CrimsonCard

    CrimsonCard is available to IU students, faculty, and staff.

  • Using your card

    CrimsonCard gives you access to every card-based university service in one convenient, secure package.

  • Partnering with CrimsonCard

    Interested in partnering with CrimsonCard to advertise your goods and services to over 200,000 cardholders statewide? Two opportunities exist and businesses can participate in one or both.
