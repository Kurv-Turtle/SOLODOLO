Undergraduate Course Facilitator Training and Resources (UCFTR)

Background image: Undergraduate Course Facilitator Training & Resources (UCFTR)

What We Do

The SLC UCFTR provides pedagogical training and resources to facilitators of the student-run Democratic Education Program (or DeCal). Through peer-led workshops and one-on-one consultation services, we aim to equip students with the skills to facilitate their classes more effectively and intentionally.

Our Philosophy

We take pride in supporting Cal undergraduates to further the decades-old legacy of DeCal. We believe that when students seize the opportunity to teach and learn from one another, they stand to transform themselves, the institution and the world at large. Thus, by equipping them with a strong theoretical and practical foundation for peer teaching, we empower Cal students to become active agents of their own learning and fulfill our mission as a conduit for academic and social transformation.
