A Culture of Service

Volunteer50 supports UC San Diego’s mission of public service by connecting our campus community with volunteer opportunities and showcasing the collective impact of our volunteers. Whether you volunteer for one of our projects, or with the nonprofit of your choice, all community service counts toward your participation in the Volunteer50 initiative.

Joining Volunteer50

Volunteering is a great way to meet new people, learn new skills and make a direct impact on your community. When you join Volunteeer50, you’ll be the first to know about upcoming UC San Diego volunteer opportunities. You’ll also enjoy these added benefits:

Volunteer50 T-Shirt

Receive a free t-shirt when you participate in a Volunteer50 service project.
