How do I upload a video to Canvas Studio?

You will learn how to upload a video to Canvas Studio for use in your course in Canvas.

1. Click on the Studio icon in Canvas.

2. Click on + Collection.

Note: This is not a necessary step but is useful, so that you can keep your videos organized by course, topic or term. However, you wish to organize them.

3. Type a title.

4. Click on Create.

5. Drag and drop your video.

Tip: You can also record your webcam or screen capture in Studio as well as upload a video from some other source. Additionally, you can link to YouTube videos which will then have a thumbnail stored in Studio.

6. Your video has now been uploaded.
