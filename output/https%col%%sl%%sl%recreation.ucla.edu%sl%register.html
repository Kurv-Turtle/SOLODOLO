Registration

Welcome to our registration page! We offer multiple registration options for all of our Recreation programs and services, both online and in-person. Browse below for important registration updates, online registration links, commonly used forms, and how to contact us.

Register for a Program or Activity

All Activities Memberships & Passes Multi-Visit Passes & 1on1 Services Book a Reservation

How to Register

  1. Visit the UCLA Recreation Program Registration web page.
  2. At the top, click on “Log In” if you have an account or “Sign Up” to create an account.
  3. Once logged in, click on “All Activities.”
  4. On the left-hand side, select "Classification" and the appropriate "Category" and correct Quarter.
  5. Click on the name of the Program Offering.
  6. Choose a section and click on REGISTER. Follow instructions to complete the transaction.
