Postings of the Week

This page features internships, jobs and related upcoming events for undergraduate and graduate students across a wide range of career fields. Opportunities featured focus on organizations with a demonstrated practice of hiring, developing and retaining diverse talent, as well as organizations owned and run by minorities and women.

In addition to basic details, most listings include information on why they are featured, including whether they were posted by alumni or are a woman- or minority-owned or run organization. Postings will be added weekly, and removed when they are closed or the application deadline has passed. 

These postings are just a small sample of the many opportunities available. You can find additional postings in Handshake, CareerShift and other sites. You can make an appointment with a career adviser to help you with your search strategy, and refer to our guides on application materials, interviews and searches for additional resources.
