Tech Briefings

Subscribe to Tech Briefings Mailing List

In September 2022, rates will change for several of the technology services provided by University IT. To view the majority of our planned rate changes for services that are broadly available to our community, please visit this page. For more information, please visit the rates section of our website.

Technology Briefings are free sessions that are open to all members of the Stanford community. The intent of these sessions is to share information about new and emerging technologies, provide vendor updates, and inform users about changes to campus technologies.

Upcoming Tech Briefings

Most sessions can be attended remotely and are recorded for later viewing. If you have an idea for a Tech Briefing topic, or have questions or comments, please submit a Help Ticket.
