          • Welcome to DAU Locations Home Page!

            The Defense Acquisition University (DAU) is responsible for the training and career development of the more than 150,000 members of the Defense Acquisition Workforce who provide weapon systems supplies and services to our nation’s warfighters. DAU uses the expertise of its people and the latest technology to deliver responsive learning assets to our customers. With our numerous locations across the United States and through our extensive virtual presence online, DAU achieves a global reach that allows us to interact with our customers anywhere, anytime.
