Outdoor Excursions

Learn skills, meet friends, reduce stress, promote inclusion and sustainability, and feed your inner desire to explore through our outdoor community.

Connect with OE on Facebook or sign up for our newsletter to stay up to date with our program offerings. Contact us at oe@ucr.edu, (951) 827-7040 for more information.
