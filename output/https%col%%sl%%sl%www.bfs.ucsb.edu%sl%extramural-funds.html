Extramural Funds

Overview

The Extramural Funds Accounting unit oversees the financial management of all external funding received by UCSB, including federal, state, Local and other government, private contracts and grants, endowments, and gifts. We advise on matters of financial compliance and proper cost accounting.  The unit provides financial reporting, cash management, accounts receivable, gift processing, effort reporting, review high risk expense transfers for compliance, cost share monitoring, and support to departments in managing Extramural funds.
