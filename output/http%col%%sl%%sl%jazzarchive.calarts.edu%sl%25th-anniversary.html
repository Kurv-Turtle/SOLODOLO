25th Anniversary Celebration

On Saturday, April 19, 2014, we celebrated the first 25 years of the collaboration between CalArts and Capitol Records.

The event was held on CalArts campus at the Wild Beast Pavilion, and featured

  • a live performance of new music from the 2014 CD,
  • presentations to Joe Smith (former President/CEO of Capitol-EMI and CalArts Board of Trustees), Paula Salvatore (Director of Capitol Studios), and some of the wonderful staff at Capitol,
