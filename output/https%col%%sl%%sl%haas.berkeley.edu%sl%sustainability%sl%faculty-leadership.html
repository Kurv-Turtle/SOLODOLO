Faculty Leadership

Berkeley Haas has a talented, award-winning faculty whose research interests span a wide range of sustainability-related topics, from energy and environmental economics to sustainable supply chains to impact investing. The faculty produce research and analysis backed by rigorous empirical evidence and the frontiers of economic research so that policy and business decisions are based on sound economic and business principles.
