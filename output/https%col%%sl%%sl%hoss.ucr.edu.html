The HOSS Will Help You Figure It Out

The Highlander One-Stop Shop (HOSS) provides many important services that will support you throughout your journey at UCR. The HOSS is a centralized space that connects students with a group of friendly and supportive HOSS Information Specialists. Get help regarding: Undergraduate Admissions (UGA), Financial Aid, Office of the Registrar and Student Business Services (SBS). If we're unable to help, we will connect you with someone who can further assist you.

We are dedicated to providing you with an efficient, effective and  stress-free experience. Come in and see us anytime — no appointments needed. 

Location & Hours

First floor of the Student Services Building (SSB), which is near Costo Hall and the Athletics & Dance Building. We are open Monday-Friday from 9 a.m.–5 p.m.
