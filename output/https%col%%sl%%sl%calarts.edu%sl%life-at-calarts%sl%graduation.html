Graduation 2022

CalArts Graduation: May 13, 2022

California Institute of the Arts (CalArts) announces that the 2022 graduation ceremony will be held in the Graduation Courtyard of the CalArts campus Friday, May 13, 2022, at 6 pm.

President’s Reception

Family and friends of the graduating class are invited to come meet with President Ravi Rajan prior to the Graduation Ceremony. Hors d'oeuvres and beverages will be served.

  • Date: Friday, May 13, 2022
  • Time: 4 pm - 5:30 pm PT
  • Location: CalArts Main Gallery
  • Open to graduates, guests, faculty, and staff.

Graduation Ceremony

Ceremony Information

  • Date: Friday, May 13 
  • Time: Graduation Procession begins promptly at 6 pm PST
  • Location: Graduation Courtyard

Post-Graduation Celebration

  • Immediately following the conclusion of the Graduation Ceremony, light refreshments will be served in the main gallery.
  • Live musical entertainment will accompany the festivities.

Frequently Asked Questions

Weather Preparations

In mid-May it's usually hot and sunny during the day, but cold when the sun goes down. Dress in layers and bring sunscreen.

Parking at the Event

There will be parking on campus and shuttles to a nearby parking lot. If you're staying near campus, ride-sharing services are encouraged.

Will food be provided?

Food will be available at Tatum Lounge until 6 p.m., leading up to the ceremony. The President's Reception also begins at 4 p.m.

Seating Arrangements

There will be chairs set up for attendees on a first come first serve basis. If seats run out, guests can sit in the grassy knolls overlooking Graduation Courtyard. Blankets, low-back lawnchairs and picnics are allowed. Blankets will also be for sale at the pop-up CalArts store. 

What's the atmosphere like at a CalArts Graduation?

Think of graduation as an outdoor party that feels more like you're watching a fun concert in the park. 

Do the graduates need a cap and gown?

CalArts does not require a cap and gown for dress code for graduation.
