International Students Programs and Activities

Language Circles

Each quarter, UCR’s International Students and Scholars office hosts a Language Circle program to help you practice your foreign language speaking skills through informal conversations with native speakers. We typically offer 6 languages: Mandarin Chinese, Korean, French, Japanese, Spanish, and English. The program is open to everyone with all levels of proficiency. Join us to practice your language speaking skills and make new friends!

R' Time

R’Time is a support network, a community and a safe space for international women at UCR to connect, find support, and practice self-care. Please see our upcoming events for information about meeting times and location.

Want to learn more? Contact Jenny Shin at jenny.shin@ucr.edu

Workshops

Throughout the year, ISS offers a variety of workshops for students on legal employment options, taxes, American classroom culture, and more! Please see our upcoming events for workshop time and location.

Volunteer Opportunities
