    Get Care

    Primary Care

    If you are enrolled in the Columbia Student Health Insurance Plan, your primary care provider is Columbia Health Medical Services. Schedule an appointment for primary care services, from wellness visits to treatment of chronic conditions.

    Urgent Concerns

    Call 212-854-7426 for 24/7 support or walk in during open hours to see a nurse for triage. Medical Services assists Columbia students with urgent medical concerns when a sudden, serious illness or injury requires immediate care.

    Medical Services Teams

    Each student has a team of Medical Services providers dedicated to their well-being: Amsterdam, Broadway, Riverside, or Morningside practice teams. You have an option to work with various physicians, nurse practitioners, and registered nurses with this team.

    Learn who the members of the different teams are on the Staff Directory. Click the Clinical Specialty filter and select Amsterdam Practice, Broadway Practice, Riverside Practice, or Morningside Practice.

    This team-based model of care is known as the Patient-Centered Medical Home (PCMH) is led by a personal clinician who provides continuous and coordinated care throughout a patient's lifetime to maximize health outcomes. The PCMH practice is responsible for providing for all of a patient’s health care needs or appropriately arranging care with other qualified professionals.
