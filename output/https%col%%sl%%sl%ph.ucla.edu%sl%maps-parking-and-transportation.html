ABOUT THE CENTER FOR HEALTH SCIENCES

The UCLA Fielding School of Public Health is located in the Center for Health Sciences (CHS) building. Offices are numbered by floor, then by corridor, then by room number. For example: 12-345 would be on Floor 1, Corridor 2, room 345. Please note that there are three floors (A, B and C) below Floor 1, with Floor C being the lowest.

  • The Dean's Office is immediately on your right once inside the Fielding School's main entrance. The room number is 16-035 CHS.
  • The Student Services Office is located on floor A, which is one floor below the first floor of the Fielding School. Once inside the the Fielding School's main entrance, proceed straight through the lobby to the elevators. Take the elevator down to floor A. Turn left out of the elevator, then take a left down the corridor. The Student Services Office will be on the left hand side in room A1-269 CHS.

If you’re heading to the UCLA Fielding School of Public Health, why not use it as a chance to do some physical activity? UCLA’s scenic campus is a great place to walk and bike, and bustling Westwood Village is a hub for public transportation.

Walking and Biking           

The Fielding School is located on the south side of Charles E. Young Drive South between Westwood  Boulevard and Tiverton Avenue.

There are several walking and biking paths that lead to the Fielding School. The interactive, searchable UCLA campus map can help you find your way around campus, and the surrounding area, on foot and by bicycle. Westwood Boulevard and Charles E. Young Drive South both feature bike lanes, and bike racks are located across the street from the Fielding School, alongside the driveway to Parking Structure 9. You can see more on this UCLA bike map.

Driving Directions to the UCLA Fielding School of Public Health

Take the 405 (San Diego Freeway) to the Wilshire Boulevard East offramp. Travel east three blocks on Wilshire Blvd. to Westwood Blvd. Turn Left on Westwood Blvd., which becomes Westwood Plaza, and continue into the campus. Turn right on Charles E. Young Drive South-- the School will be on your right.

Driving Directions From Los Angeles International Airport (LAX)

Take the 405 (San Diego Freeway) northbound to Wilshire Boulevard East, and continue as described above.

Parking      

Guest parking can be found on the roof of Parking Structure 8, located at the southwest corner of Westwood Boulevard and Strathmore Drive. After parking, you must purchase a ticket at a self-service parking pay station. The pay stations offer a variety of parking options, and are located at various points along the edge of the parking lot. After paying at the station, take the ticket and leave it face-up on the dashboard of your car where it can be visible. Be sure not to park in any space marked as "Permit Only" parking.

To reach the Fielding School from Parking Structure 8, head south on Westwood Boulevard toward Charles E. Young Drive South. Turn left on Charles E. Young Drive South. The Fielding School will be on the right hand side.
