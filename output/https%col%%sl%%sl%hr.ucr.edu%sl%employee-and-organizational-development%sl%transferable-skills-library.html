  • Transferable Skills Library

 

 

The Transferable Skills Library provides employees an opportunity to become more effective in
their current roles or develop the skills needed for a future role on campus. 

Developed by UC Riverside’s Human Resources Employee and Organizational Development (EOD) team, the Transferable Skill Library (TSL) features the most popular on-demand courses from LinkedIn Learning using 20 job standard skillsets categorized by UCR’s Core Competency Behavioral Anchors.  

As TSL expands you will have access to skill related articles, e-Courses, videos and additional resources at your fingertips by utilizing an electronic device (e.g., computer, tablet, smartphone) from anywhere in the world.

Please note: While these are the most transferable skills across positions, each job is unique, and a mastery of skills alone may not be enough to transition to a new position. Visit My UC Career to access tools for assessing your preferences, skills and temperament.

UCR Core Competency Anchors: Achieving Results, Building Relationships, Creating the Future

 

The 20 job standard skillsets are aligned with the core behavioral anchors which apply to all UC employees, regardless of location, level or role within the University. Visit the EOD Course Catalog to learn more about Behavioral Anchors and Core Competencies.

How to Use

Decide on the behavioral anchor or the specific skill you would like to learn. Click on the skill header to see the on-demand courses -> click the course of interest you would like to learn from.

LinkedIn Learning

LinkedIn Learning is an online course provider, offering on-demand video courses taught by industry experts in software, creative, and business skills. Sign in via R’Space to activate your account.

Jhana

Jhana is an online manager reference tool that provides, templates, and tips that address hundreds of common management challenges. It can help you solve problems, build skills, and coach others. Jhana is available to those with a Franklin Covey All Access Pass (AAP) account. For information on how to get an AAP account, contact hrtrainingcontacts@ucr.edu.
