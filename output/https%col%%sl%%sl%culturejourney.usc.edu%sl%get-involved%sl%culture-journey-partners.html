Culture Journey Partners

We want to provide an opportunity for our Trojan family to join us in this meaningful work with the Culture Journey. If you are interested in being notified about upcoming events, panels, or forums or just want to stay in the loop, consider being a Culture Journey Partner.

Take the next step by becoming a Culture Journey Partner

  • Receive monthly newsletters from the centralized program including updates, culture activation spotlights, and opportunities to participate in ongoing culture activities based on availability and interest
  • Can choose to join quarterly culture journey spotlight calls to stay informed about the USC culture journey and learn more about living out our unifying values
