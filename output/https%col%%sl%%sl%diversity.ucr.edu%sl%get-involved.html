Brown Bag Conversations


Join the conversation about the value of differences in the workplace while advancing diversity, equity and inclusion in our community.

These Brown Bags hope to provide professional development, networking and outreach opportunities for campus members.

Our monthly virtual Brown Bag conversations may have thematic foci but no necessarily planned agendas, just conversation and networking among those who participate.

We look forward to hearing your thoughts and ideas.
