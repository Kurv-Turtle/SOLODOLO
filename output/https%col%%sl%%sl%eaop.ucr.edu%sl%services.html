Services

 

Winning Strategies for Winning Futures

EAOP specializes in academic advising that helps students stay motivated and on track to complete the “a-g” course requirements required for UC and CSU admissions eligibility. All services are based on the program's four strategies: Academic Enrichment, Entrance Exams, Academic Advising and College Knowledge. The services are designed to enhance and supplement the academic work available to the student. Learn more about the types of services offered by EAOP at UC Riverside below.

Freshman (9th)

  • "a–g" Requirements
  • Individual Academic Plan (IAP)
  • The UC System, CSU, CC and Private Colleges
  • Note-Taking and Study Strategies
  • Goal Setting
  • Test-Taking Skills

Sophomore (10th)

  • "a–g" Requirements
  • Individual Academic Plan (IAP)
  • Grade Point Calculation
  • Note-Taking and Study Strategies
  • Major and Career Exploration
  • PSAT, SAT and ACT Info

Junior (11th)

  • Campus Tours
  • Transcript Evaluation
  • SAT/ACT Preparation
  • Financial Aid
  • Choosing a College
  • College Application Development

Senior (12th)

  • College Applications
  • Transcript Evaluation
  • Choosing a College and a Major
  • Completing the FAFSA
  • Personal Statement
  • Transition to College
