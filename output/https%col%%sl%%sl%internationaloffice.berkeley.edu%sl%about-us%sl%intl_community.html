International Community at Cal

International Students 

Berkeley International Office hosts more than 6,000 degree seeking and visiting students. BIO compiles annual statistical reports as well  as assessments about our international student population. For services and resources provided to students, see information for Students.

Visiting International Scholars

Berkeley International Office compiles annual reports on visiting international professors and researchers by field, immigration status and more.  See also our information about J-1 Exchange Visitors.

International Families

Resources for accompanying family members including schools, work permission for eligible 
J-2 dependents, English language programs, legal matters and more.
