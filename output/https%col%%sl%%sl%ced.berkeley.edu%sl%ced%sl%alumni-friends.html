Alumni + Friends

As alumni and supporters, you are integral to our success, and we are deeply grateful for your continued interest in CED. We offer several ways for you to remain part of the CED community, with its unparalleled academic programs, remarkable students, and enriching events and activities. As an alumnus/a or friend you can participate through giving, networking opportunities, reunions, our lecture series, exhibitions, the Berkeley Circus, mentorship for current students, and review panels. Your participation in college events enriches the experience for faculty, staff, and students.

Renee Chow, Acting Dean
