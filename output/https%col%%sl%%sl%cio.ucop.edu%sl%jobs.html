Jobs in IT at the University of California

There are many open positions in IT across the UC locations at any one time. The UC Office of the President hosts a website where you can search for all IT jobs at any or all locations. For example, try using the keyword “information technology,” or a job title, and select All Locations.

Occasionally, a campus CIO will feature a particular open position (see below). These positions are posted here for one to two months. Notification about them is sent out via the subscription newsletter for the UC IT Blog.

Lead for Diversity, Equity, Inclusion and Belonging – Berkeley IT

UC Berkeley IT has an exciting position in the Office of the CIO – the Lead for Diversity, Equity, Inclusion and Belonging. The individual will lead the development and implementation of strategic and proactive initiatives that promote diversity, equity, inclusion, and belonging for Berkeley IT, with a focus on creating a strong and inclusive culture, improving the employee experience for underrepresented and historically marginalized staff, and supporting UC Berkeley as a place where people want to work.

For more information, Michelle Marquez, Director of People, Performance and Culture, Berkeley IT.
