Contact Us

HR staff members are reachable by phone and email. Call (609) 258-3300 or email using the form below or hr@princeton.edu for assistance. Individuals who need to visit the HR main office can make an appointment Mondays through Fridays during work hours by calling (609) 258-3300 or emailing hr@princeton.edu.

Employees can update personal information, including mailing address, emergency contact information, mobile phone number, and email address, through HR Self Service.
