Faculty and Staff

UC San Diego faculty and staff prefer the path less traveled. We lead by example through our personal commitment to the work we do and by supporting an ever-evolving and always improving UC San Diego experience with our philanthropic investments.

“I am deeply committed to UC San Diego’s mission. As staff co-chair of the Faculty and Staff Giving Council; I want to enable, as well as highlight faculty and staff who give back to support our mission to transform California and a diverse global society by educating, by generating and disseminating knowledge and creative works, and by engaging in public service.”

– Dr. LaWana Richmond, EdD ’15 Faculty and Staff Giving Council, Staff Co-Chair

Every gift of any amount to any fund counts!

All faculty and staff gifts help UC San Diego achieve excellence! By supporting your passion with an annual gift, you’ll be joining fellow faculty and staff donors in the Campaign for UC San Diego.

Your Passion!

When you support your passion, you’re helping transform the student experience, our campus, and ultimately humanity.

Your Gift!

By making a gift, you express your belief in UC San Diego and support our world-renowned university. You may designate your gift of any amount to support any fund.

Your Impact!

You enhance the student experience; enrich our campus community; spark research and innovation, and show that you care about UC San Diego.

“I give back to the educational mission of the UC San Diego School of Medicine and the Internal Medicine Residency Program. As a healthcare provider at UC San Diego Health, I see daily the direct positive impact faculty and staff have on patients and their families. I know that all across campus, faculty and staff carry out the University’s mission to create and disseminate knowledge to the next generation of leaders.”

– WILLIAM J. FREDERICK III, MD, PHD, PHYSICIAN ADVISOR, DEPARTMENT OF CARE MANAGEMENT AND HEALTH SCIENCES ASSOCIATE CLINICAL PROFESSOR, DEPARTMENT OF INTERNAL MEDICINE
