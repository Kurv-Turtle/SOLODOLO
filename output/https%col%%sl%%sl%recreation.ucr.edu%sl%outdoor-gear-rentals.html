Outdoor Gear Rentals

Snowboards, surfboards and camping equipment – we’ll outfit you for an extraordinary day on the slopes, in the water or at the campsite.

The UCR Student Recreation Center's Outdoor Excursions Shop has an extensive library of geographical maps and outdoor gear for your next adventure! Students, staff or community members – rental equipment is available to anyone age 18 and over with a valid ID. UCR students must show a current UCR ID at the time of reservation or rental. Non-UCR students must show a valid government-issued ID. A client can only rent two like items at a time.

Contact us at oe@ucr.edu, (951) 827-7040 for more information.

Rental Details

Before heading out on your outdoor activity, please review all rental details below. Rates and policies are subject to change without notice.

 

 

Rates & Discounts

 

 

When an item is checked out, it is due back to the OE shop by closing on the day it is scheduled to be returned. A 20% discount is given for rentals lasting four or more days. We do not charge for days the OE Shop is closed (i.e. holidays/campus closures).

 

 

Reservations

 

 

Equipment rental reservations can be made in person at the UCR Student Recreation Center's Outdoor Excursions Shop, by email (oe@ucr.edu), or by phone (951- 827-7040). Payment for equipment is due at the time of pick up.

 

 

Late Fees

 

 

A late fee (the daily rate x2) will be charged for each day an item is overdue. We do not waive late fees. Items that are one week overdue – without communication from the renter – are considered lost. A replacement fee and any lost revenue will be assessed.

 

 

Refunds

 

 

Only cancellations made by OE will result in a full refund. No refunds or credits will be made due to weather, road conditions or unclaimed/unused/early-return equipment.

 

 

Responsibility

 

 

The person who checked out rental gear is responsible for each item (based on inventory number) in their name. Cleaning or damage fees may be assessed if equipment is returned dirty, wet, or in poor condition.

 

 

Damage/Replacement

 

 

A replacement fee will be assessed for any lost, stolen or irreparable equipment. The OE staff will determine the amount owed. The maximum fee is the full retail value of the equipment.
