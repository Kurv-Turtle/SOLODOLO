Recognition and Awards

Recognition and Awards

Honoring Commitment
Each year, the Alumni Association celebrates the invaluable contributions volunteers make to MIT and to your fellow alumni by recognizing award recipients in all categories of volunteering for the Institute. Their excellence is representative of the service provided MIT by alumni volunteers, and provides inspiration for others to get involved.

  • Annual Leadership Awards

    Learn more about these annual awards that honor outstanding service to the Alumni Association and MIT. 

     

  • Submit an Annual Award Nomination

    Help the Association honor the extraordinary contributions of volunteers. All alumni are invited to nominate volunteers for recognition.

  • Volunteer Honor Roll of Service

    The Honor Roll recognizes the volunteer contributions of alumni, parents, widows, spouses, faculty, and current student volunteers.

  • Alumni Leadership Conference

    Join the MIT volunteer community for this annual gathering of alumni leadership. ALC offers you learning, inspiration, skills enhancement, and recognition. Find out more.

Submit a Volunteer Honor Roll Nomination
