About this blog

“Notes from the Quad” offers periodic thoughts from Stanford President Marc Tessier-Lavigne and Provost Persis Drell on current issues at Stanford. Guest posts from other campus leaders will appear here from time to time, as well.

You can subscribe using the form in the right sidebar to receive email alerts when there are new posts. Contact president(at)stanford.edu or provost(at)stanford.edu with your thoughts and feedback.

About Marc Tessier-Lavigne

Pioneering neuroscientist, biotech executive and academic leader, Marc Tessier-Lavigne became Stanford University’s eleventh president in September 2016.
president.stanford.edu

About Persis Drell

As the chief academic and budgetary officer of Stanford, Provost Drell oversees the university’s instructional and research programs and coordinates campus administrative and support functions with academic purposes.
provost.stanford.edu
