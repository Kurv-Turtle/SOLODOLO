Congratulations, Class of 2022!

L&S departments score high in the 2023 Best Graduate Schools rankings recently released by U.S. News & World Report

The College of Letters & Science (L&S) is the great, big heart of UC Berkeley: great because our international stature is the core of the university’s intellectual preeminence, and big because we are the largest of Berkeley’s colleges, comprising 38 extraordinary departments and offering more than 80 diverse majors. Despite our size, L&S is a place where students discover the curriculum they want and the community they need.

For Students

Our rigorous academic programs are second to none, and our capacity for impact far exceeds that of our private peers — every year 6,000 talented young people graduate from our college, ready to lead change for the greater good.
