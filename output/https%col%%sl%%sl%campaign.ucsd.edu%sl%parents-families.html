Experiences Beyond the Classroom

Your support for the Parents Fund impacts all students by providing resources to foster a balance of academics, personal growth and extracurricular activities that transform your student’s experience.

Campus Events and Cultural Activities

The Parents Fund provides your student the opportunity to enhance their out-of-the-classroom experience by listening to guest speakers and attending cultural events on campus.

Career Development Opportunities

The Parents Fund provides students access to career development opportunities, conferences, and workshops to prepare to build their skill-set.

Parents and families love to give to these areas

We encourage our families to give across campus in support of their personal passions. Every annual gift is meaningful and will enhance the student experience.

Parent Leadership Council

Council members are university advocates who enrich the student experience through personal involvement, philanthropic support and fiduciary responsibility for the designations of the Parents Fund.

Triton Parent and Family Ambassador Program

Current parent and family volunteers serve as ambassadors to provide a one-on-one connection to help prospective and incoming families.

Family Events

Parent and Family Programs engages family members as partners. Expand your UC San Diego and reconnect with your student through these campus-wide events.
