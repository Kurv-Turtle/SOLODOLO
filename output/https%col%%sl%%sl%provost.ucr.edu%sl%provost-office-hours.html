OFFICE HOURS

Provost Elizabeth Watkins holds weekly office hours to get to know and hear from faculty, staff, and students at UCR. Instead of gathering in a large forum, office hours are opportunities to chat one-on-one or in small groups. Campus members can bring any questions or concerns to the meeting or use the meeting time to share perspectives and ideas. 

The office hours are offered by campus affiliation on a rotating basis, one week for staff, the next for faculty, the following for students, and so on. You can sign up for a 15-minute time slot below. 

For your first time registering for office hours, follow the link to the time slot you'd like below and then simply click "Login/Create Account" and register for your time slot once you are logged in. 

*Note* Office Hours will begin meeting primarily in-person starting September 1st. The Provost's Office is on the 4th Floor of Hinderaker Hall (Suite 4148). If a remote/Zoom option is needed, one will be provided closer to your appointment date.
