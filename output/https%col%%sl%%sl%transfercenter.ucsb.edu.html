COVID Campus Updates

Dear campus community,
 
The health and wellbeing of our community is of the utmost importance to the TSC Team. As permitted, the TSC is offering in-person services during Spring 2022. Our team is working Monday - Friday between 9 am - 5 pm PST.  College of Letters and Science Academic Advising is offered on a mostly remote basis. To meet with an Academic Advisor on drop-in, please use Q-less.

Keep Learning Website and Resources

Are you looking for resources to support your remote instruction experience? Visit UCSB's Keep Learning website for indepth remote learning resources and tips!  You can expect to find effective strategies for remote learning, Zoom tips & suggestions, information on Campus Learning Assistance Services (CLAS), academic advising, internet access & technology help, and more.
 
If you would like to chat with a peer about any of the topics on the Keep Learning website, visit our Peer Mentors or Graduate Student Mentor during their drop-in hours!

Community and Resources for UCSB Transfer Students:

Transfer Student Center is a space for UCSB's transfer students to make connections: with each other, to campus resources, and with pathways to academic and career success. The Center is home to two full-time academic advisors from the College of Letters & Science, peer mentors, and departmental partners who are able to help transfer students transition successfully to UCSB by providing specialized programming and opportunities that add value to their undergraduate education. We are open Monday-Friday, 9am-5pm.

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ZcmVuE_7n80

 

 

Use Nectir to connect digitally at anytime. Designed by recent UCSB grads, Nectir is a communication tool UCSB adopted this year in order to create online communities on campus. Through either your desktop or your phone, you can now join a community of UCSB Transfer students in our #transfers19 Nectir channel. This will be a space to ask and answer each others’ questions, share tips and tricks, and find support from your peers. Whether you have insights about the transfer experiences, questions about programs the TSC offers we would love to hear from you!

The conversation has already begun, and it's as easy as 1-2-3:

  1. Create an account using your UCSB.edu email in the Nectir box above by clicking "Register New Account." 
  2.  Begin chatting
  3.  Ask and answer each others' questions, share events, and get support from peer and staff transfer peer educators and advisors
