 Mission

              
​ The MIT Parent Leadership Circle is a group of committed parents who:

              
  1. build parent connection

              
  2. engage in opportunities to learn about MIT research and student life, and

              
  3. impact MIT through philanthropy.

              
 

              
 

              
MIT Parent Leadership Circle

              
Through annual giving, Parent Leadership Circle (PLC) parents help support MIT initiatives that are meaningful to their families and align with MIT’s purpose to make a better world through education, research and innovation.

              
Membership in the PLC provides parents with opportunities to attend on-campus, regional and virtual events that deepen connection to the Institute.  

              

              
Parent Leadership Circle annual giving levels: 

              
              Builder                     $10,000+

              
              Maker                       $2,500 - $9,999

              
 

              

               
“We are proud to participate in the Parent Leadership Circle which supports our students and MIT’s mission to create a better world. The PLC connected us to fellow parents and the broader MIT community, and gave us a window into the work of the Institute and the student experience.”

               
Nicole and David Gruenstein, P '21

Honor your students' graduation from MIT

             
With an honorary gift to MIT, you are able to recognize your students' milestone of graduation from MIT. Honorary or celebratory gifts can be made to an existing MIT fund, or by establishing a named fund for a specific purpose.
