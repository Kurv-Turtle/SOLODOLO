Academic Approach

Our core and elective coursework is focused on leadership development and analytical rigor. Berkeley Haas emphasizes the study and application of key functional business drivers and the breadth of a general management approach.

CFOs need to understand competitive marketing dynamics. CMOs need to understand the financial drivers of the business. At Berkeley Haas, our graduates know that understanding a complex world requires broad analytic skills AND emotional intelligence. Hard skills and soft skills working together to help you achieve great things personally and professionally.

All Berkeley Haas programs are structured to provide an integrated view of business and prepare students to manage, lead, and solve complex business problems.
