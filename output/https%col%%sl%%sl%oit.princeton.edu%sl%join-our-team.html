Join Our Team

As Princeton University's central IT organization, the Office of Information Technology provides IT services to a community of more than 15,000 faculty, students, and staff. With more than 320 staff, the OIT team delivers on more than 230 services that range from programming, infrastructure engineering, and technical support to IT consulting, finance, and administration.
