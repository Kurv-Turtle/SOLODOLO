Provost Office Hours (Staff)

Provost Elizabeth Watkins holds regular office hours to get to know
and hear from faculty, staff, and students at UCR. Instead of gathering in a large forum, office hours are opportunities to chat one-on-one or in small groups. Campus members can bring any questions or
concerns to the meeting or use the meeting time to share perspectives and ideas.

The office hours are offered by campus affiliation on a rotating basis, one week for staff, the next for faculty, the following for students, and so on. You can check the schedule and sign up for a 15-minute time slot at provost.ucr.edu/provost-office-hours

*Note* Office Hours will be primarily in person, on the 4th Floor of Hinderaker Hall (Suite 4148). For those who need to attend remotely, a Zoom option with be available.
