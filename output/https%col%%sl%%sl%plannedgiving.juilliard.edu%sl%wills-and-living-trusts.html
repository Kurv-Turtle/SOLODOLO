A Gift in Your Will or Living Trust

Deepen Your Connections

A gift through your will or living trust is an important way to make a big impact at The Juilliard School. By making a gift this way, you also have the flexibility to make changes in the event that life circumstances change.

You can make a real difference for young artists with as little as one sentence. This type of donation to Juilliard through your will or living trust helps ensure that the School can continue to educate the world's finest young performing artists for generations to come.

See How It Works

An Example of How It Works

As a lover of the performing arts, Juilliard had long been a part of Susanâ€™s life. When she and her husband Bob created their wills, they decided to include a $100,000 gift to Juilliard to help train future artists. As their family grew to include grandchildren, Susan and Bob decided to revise their gift to ensure their grandchildren's future financial security.

They met with their attorney and revised the gift language so that Juilliard received a percentage of their estate, instead of a specific amount. Susan and Bob now have the satisfaction of knowing their plans will provide for the people they love, and for the charitable work most important to them.
