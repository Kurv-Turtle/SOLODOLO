College of the Canyons

Institutional Scholarship Program
 

The application for the 2022-2023 academic year will OPEN on 2/9/2022. The application DEADLINE will be 3/23/2022.

The College of the Canyons Institutional Scholarship Program is an annual scholarship program created only for College of the Canyons students.  The program offers a great variety of scholarships based upon academic achievement and/or financial need.  Criteria vary from scholarship to scholarship.  Funding for the annual scholarship program comes from  two major partners - the COC Foundation and Associated Student Government. Gifts and endowments from community organizations, private individuals, corporations as well as foundations, sponsor student scholarships as a means of expressing confidence in College of the Canyons students.

ELIGIBILITY REQUIREMENTS: Students will be screened for scholarship eligibility based on criteria established for each individual scholarship. You must:

           - Have completed a minimum of 6 units prior to Spring semester
           - Have at least a 2.00 cumulative grade point average
           - Be currently enrolled at least part-time (6+ units)

If you would like further information and additional scholarship opportunities, please visit our website at http://www.canyons.edu/offices/FinAid/scholarships.asp.

Remember, privately funded scholarships are only one form of financial aid available to students.  If you would like to be considered for other types of financial aid, please contact the Financial Aid Office at (661) 362-3215 or finaid@canyons.edu or visit the website at  www.canyons/money4college for further information.
