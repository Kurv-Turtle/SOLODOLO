2010 Conference Alumni Events Leadership

Mark Rittenberg on the art of leadership presence

 

[1 hour 1 min] Today’s managers are challenged to be more productive with reduced resources, especially with fewer employees. How then can a good leader achieve breakthrough results?

In this 2010 All-Alumni Conference session, popular CEE instructor Mark Rittenberg introduces participants to three important, yet often overlooked areas of leadership: identifying talent; empowering and motivating people; and deepening expertise throughout an organization. These are the skills that distinguish artful leaders and enable organizations to thrive.
