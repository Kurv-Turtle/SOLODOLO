About

Through academic, professional development, and community engagement opportunities, Cornell in Washington gives you the chance to turn knowledge into action. Choose from fall, spring, and summer options, intern, and earn Cornell credit.

Why study in Washington?

The entire city becomes your campus; the museums, the history, the nightlife, and the political atmosphere provide an experience unlike anything Cornell's Ithaca campus has to offer.
