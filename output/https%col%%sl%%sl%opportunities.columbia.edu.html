    Manager-Finance & Ops Columbia Journalism Review Morningside
    Reporting jointly to the Editor and Publisher of the Columbia Journalism Review (CJR) and the Director of Finance, the role of the Operations Manager is to manage the finances and administrative operations for CJR. The Operations Manager will ensure that CJR is compliant with all University and Journalism School budgeting and financial regulations and processes. Working closely with the Publisher and Managing Editor, the Operations Manager will also help support CJR's earned revenue and fundraising activities. This position is contingent upon continued funding.
    Manager-Finance/Academic Affs Graduate School of Journalism Morningside

    Administrative Assistant Lamont-Doherty Earth Observatory Lamont-Doherty Earth Observatory
    The Lamont-Doherty Earth Observatory campus of Columbia University located in Palisades, New York, is seeking a full-time Administrative Assistant to provide clerical support for the Building and Grounds department and for some ancillary services that are overseen by the Facilities department. The Administrative Assistant will directly support the Manager of the department on a day-to-day basis in all departmental operations and will maintain Buildings and Grounds records and contracts.
    Administrative Assistant Lamont-Doherty Earth Observatory Lamont-Doherty Earth Observatory
    The Lamont-Doherty Earth Observatory campus of Columbia University located in Palisades, New York, is seeking an Administrative Assistant to provide support to the Ocean and Climate Physics department. Provides direct support in the office management and budget preparation and proposal assistance to scientists in an active research group. Maintains all records, both electronic and hard file, and is responsible for reports and related deadlines.
    Financial Analyst Lamont-Doherty Earth Observatory Lamont-Doherty Earth Observatory
    The Lamont-Doherty Earth Observatory of Columbia University located in Palisades, New York, is seeking a Financial Analyst for a full-time opportunity. The Financial Analyst will provide support to Senior Management in carrying out financial and accounting responsibilities of the Observatory's Office of Marine Operations (OMO).
    Administrative Assistant Department of Slavic Languages Morningside

    Assistant Director, Advising and Academic Success in the Office of Student Affairs Columbia Business School Manhattanville
    The Office of Student Affairs at Columbia Business School supports the academic and community experience for 1600 full-time MBA students. The Office of Student Affairs is committed to creating an inclusive environment in which all students feel a sense of belonging, agency and partnership. The Assistant Director is responsible for providing academic management and academic success services to MBA students and will serve as the point person for the Peer Tutoring Program, and Knight Bagehot Fellow’s program on the Academics Team. The Academic Advisor also works directly to manage projects with the Academics Team, the Office of Student Affairs (OSA), and conducts special projects as needed.
    Program Manager, Healthcare and Pharmaceutical Management Program Columbia Business School Manhattanville
    The purpose of the Healthcare and Pharmaceutical Management Program is to provide Columbia Business School students with the knowledge, understanding and relationships to successfully contribute to the healthcare industry as business professionals who can anticipate and solve the complex, global economic and social problems the industry is facing. Reporting to the Faculty Director, the Program Manager is responsible for the administrative coordination and management of the Healthcare & Pharmaceutical Management Program, focused on educating future business leaders in healthcare; mentoring students and alumni on career opportunities in the healthcare industry; collaborating with schools within the University; engaging with industry leaders on program initiatives; and developing thought leadership in the space.
    Variable Hours Officer - Technical Support Specialist School of Social Work Morningside
    Reporting to the Instructional Technology Manager at the Columbia University School of Social Work’s Office of Computing and Instructional Technology, and working closely with the Assistant Director, the Technical Support Specialist (TSS), will be responsible for providing technical support to instructors in an online and hyflex classroom environment (on-campus support) as well as online and hyflex events hosted by School. The candidate will provide guidance and support on the use of instructional teaching tools such as polls, slides, videos, breakout rooms, and other assets. The TSS will also provide technical support and guidance before, during, and after each class session for the course they are assigned and any events that may be in-person, online or hyflex.
    Chef Manager Campus Services Morningside

    Business Development Assoc Columbia Technology Ventures Morningside
    The Business Development Associate will report to the Assoc. Director for Business Development & Portfolio management and will support CTV’s Licensing Team on commercializing highest-impact technologies, growing relationships with prospective licensees, identifying and developing new academic-industry research alliances and overall portfolio management. Specifically, the Business Development Associate will work closely with the Assoc. Director for BD to support the growing portfolio of life science focused industry alliances as well as develop and manage relationships with industry and investors.
    Subaward Specialist Sponsored Projects Admin Medical Center
    Under the supervision of the Manager of Contract Negotiations and guidance of the Senior Subaward Officer, the Subaward Specialist operates as a member of the Sponsored Project Administration (SPA) Subawards unit, which is responsible for more than 1,500 subaward actions issued yearly for externally funded grants and contracts from government and non-government sponsors. The Subaward Specialist exercises professional judgment in the issuance, negotiation and execution process of a subaward and/or subcontract.
    Education Coordinator Medical Humanities and Ethics Medical Center

    Assistant to Head of School, K-8 School The School at Columbia Univ Morningside
    The School at Columbia University is seeking an Assistant to the Head to provide a wide range of managerial and administrative support to the Head of School and Director’s Group. The position will report to the Head of School.
    Coordinator-Enrollment The School at Columbia Univ Morningside
    The School at Columbia works to support placement in a wide variety of independent, public, boarding and parochial schools.
    Assistant Provost for Academic Appointments Office of the Provost Morningside
    Reporting to the Associate Provost for Academic Appointments, the Assistant Provost for Academic Appointments is responsible for the approval of Morningside academic personnel appointments, reappointments, promotions, leaves, terminations, and additional compensation ; for the approval of Visiting Scholar/Scientist designations; for overseeing all leave requests and phased retirement agreements for academic personnel throughout the University; for the verification of academic transactions in the University’s People @ Columbia system; for maintaining tenure-track and nontenured faculty records; for advising schools and departments on policies relating to academic appointments and for managing special projects related to academic personnel. The Assistant Provost engages in and contributes to strategic planning for the Division of Academic Appointments.
