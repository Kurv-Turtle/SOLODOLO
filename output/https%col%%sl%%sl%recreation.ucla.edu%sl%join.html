UCLA Students

All UCLA students (undergraduate, graduate, Anderson, Law, and Dental) who are currently enrolled in summer quarter and pay student service fees toward Recreation, will automatically obtain summer quarter membership beginning June 10, 2022. No membership registration is required.

The following UCLA students DO NOT pay student service fees and therefore need to pay the membership fee:

  • Students who graduated spring quarter
  • Students who are not enrolled in summer quarter classes
  • UCLA Extension students enrolled in the summer quarter
  • Students from self-supporting degree programs

Pricing: $60/quarter (prorated beginning late-July)

Registration Process
Instructions:

1. Go to: https://secure.recreation.ucla.edu/membership/index

2. Login to your account:

• UCLA faculty, staff, and students: use the blue bar “click here for UCLA logon”

• Other users: enter your credentials in the blank field “email or username”

3. Choose “|Rec Membership|”

4. Select the duration by clicking the grey “1 quarter” bar, then use the drop-down to select “Summer 2022”

5. Confirm the dates of membership and ensure the expiration is September 30, 2022.

6. Proceed through the checkout process

Important: If you are an eligible affiliate described above and see “Active Membership Required” or are unable to register, please email info@recreation.ucla.edu with your UCLA ID numberand a screenshot of your enrolled program or description of your current affiliation to activate your Recreation account so you can be eligible to purchase a student membership.

Payroll Deduction Policies

Payroll deduction membership must be paid upfront for the current quarter. Your payroll deductions will begin on the 1st of the following quarter.

For Example: Pay upfront $90/spring quarter (prorated); Payroll Deduction begins July 1/summer quarter

ELIGIBILITY:

Payroll Deduction is a privilege for UCLA Faculty and Staff purchasing ongoing Recreation membership, locker rental, Group X Pass, BHIP, and Youth & Family summer programs.

Payroll Deduction is only available for:

  1. CAREER, CONTRACTED, OR ACADEMIC EMPLOYEES (or)
  2. TEMPORARY EMPLOYEES with end dates who work a minimumof 75% time

All other employees must pay for their services in full at the time of purchase. (We are unable to extend payroll deduction to ASUCLA Employees.)

RECREATION SERVICES: Recreation Services‐ fitness passes, BHIP, and locker rentals ‐ may be added subsequent to the primary member’s enrollment and added to payroll deduction. Payroll Deduction updates/changes must be submitted by the 10thof the month to be effective for the following month.

CANCELLATION: Payroll Deduction is perpetual and will continue until canceled. Payroll Deduction must be canceled in writing via the online form (see below) and timestamped by the 10th day of the month to cancel your deduction for the following pay period. Employees must cancel payroll deductions prior to going on temporary leave or ending appointment with UCLA, or you will automatically get additional charges (non-refundable) upon return to any UC location.

Cancellation Request form: https://uclacampuslife.formstack.com/workflows/cancellation

Policies & COVID Acknowledgments

  • Facilities will have reduced capacities and limited hours.
  • Facilities may also have intermittent and immediate closures due to health and cleaning requirements.
  • Reservations may be canceled and facilities closed in inclement weather, unsafe conditions, and for scheduled classes.
  • Personal water bottle recommended. Beverages must be in a spill-proof, unbreakable, re-sealable container. No spitting.
  • Maintain personal hygiene as to not be disruptive to the environment.
  • Standing on or leaning over rails, fences, and other equipment is prohibited.
  • No photography or video recording unless approved by UCLA Rec management.
  • Be respectful of all equipment, patrons, and staff.
  • Follow all staff directions, participant expectations, and posted facility signage.

Entry Policies

  • All individuals must complete the required UCLA Covid-19 Symptom Monitoring Survey prior to coming to campus. UCLA students/staff/faculty must use the UCLA mobile app to complete the survey: UCLA mobile app
  • Present current Rec Card, Bruincard, valid photo ID, or UCLA Rec phone app barcode for entry.
  • Indoor masking is required regardless of vaccination status.
  • UCLA Campus COVID-19 policy requires that Recreation members are required to conduct weekly surveillance testing (and no more than 7 days apart) for COVID-19, regardless of vaccination status.
  • Maintain 6 feet of distance from others or more as determined by facility or program.
  • Secured storage is not available. Users are responsible for personal belongings. Do not bring valuables. UCLA Recreation is not responsible for lost or stolen items.
  • Spaces must be used as intended. No loitering.
  • Any participant displaying symptoms of illness may be asked to leave the facility.
  • You may not enter the zone before your Bookings start time and you must exit the facility by your Bookings end time.
  • Failure to comply with policies and guidelines, facility signage, and staff instruction may result in removal from a facility, a membership suspension, or membership revocation.

Participant Expectations

UCLA Recreation believes it is essential that all people exercise their right to express their human and cultural identities. Consistent with this belief, our programs promote healthy lifestyles and the preservation of human dignity by fostering the spirit of fair play without bigotry, hatred, prejudice, or disrespect. We encourage you to join us in acknowledging and embracing these values. Review the UCLA Recreation Participant Expectations prior to accessing our facilities.

COVID Acknowledgement of Risks

Prior to entering any UCLA Recreation venues or participating in any Recreation program (including club and intramural sports), we ask that you fully understand the potential risks associated with contraction of the SARS-CoV-2 virus, which causes the COVID-19 disease. Contraction of the virus could cause serious illness or death. For more information, please visit the following resources:

  • Bruin Safe Online
  • Covid-19 UCLA
  • CDC Coronavirus 2019
  • Coronavirus LA FAQ
  • LA County DPH Coronavirus
  • California COVID-19 Industry Guidance


To help minimize the risk of transmission of the virus within our venues and programs, we have implemented the following safety protocols.

  • Surveillance testing and daily health screening for all staff, as well as required face coverings for staff while working and at all times when in the presence of others.
  • Enhanced training for staff regarding public health mitigation measures that must be followed to reduce likelihood of transmission of COVID-19.
  • Posted signage, floor markings, and staff instructions to ensure physical distancing, both within our venues (6 feet) and also while participating in programs (6-10 feet).
  • Reduced venue and program capacity via a reservation process, and also by limiting access to registered students, faculty, and staff who are engaged in active recreation programs or activities.
  • Enhanced disinfecting of high-touch areas and shared equipment.
  • Increased availability of hand sanitizing stations throughout our venues.
  • In venues where it is possible, separate entry and exit points.


To further minimize the risk of transmission of COVID-19, and to abide by current University, County, and/or State guidelines, we will require all individuals entering our venues (including those entering to participate in programs) to:

  • Only visit with a confirmed reservation or confirmed program registration.
  • Honestly complete a daily Symptom Monitoring Questionnaire and do not enter the campus or facility if not cleared or experiencing a cough, fever, or symptoms of COVID-19. Clearance certificates will be requested prior to entry of any Recreation facility.
  • Abide by physical distancing signs, floor markings, and staff instructions including use of the provided sanitization supplies to sanitize any equipment that you may utilize during your workout.
  • Indoor masking is required for those who are not up-to-date with their COVID-19 vaccinations.

I understand that no list of restrictions, guidelines, or practices can eliminate the risk of exposure to the virus that causes COVID-19. I further understand that I play a crucial role in keeping everyone in the venue and program safer during the pandemic. I understand that failure to do so may result in loss of UCLA Recreation privileges, as well as potential further disciplinary action.

Booking Policies

Booking Instructions:

  1. Go to: https://secure.recreation.ucla.edu/booking
  2. Choose desired facility and available timeslot

Bookings open 72 hours in advance of each timeslot. Bookings are not guaranteed.

  • Must be an eligible UCLA affiliate with an active student membership or Recreation membership to make a Bookings. No guest or day passes are allowed.
  • Bookings are required to enter certain Recreation spaces unless enrolled in a Recreation in-person program or class.
  • Participants will be able to make a Booking starting 72 hours in advance, up until one hour prior to the Booking start time. By obtaining this membership a Booking is not guaranteed at any time.
  • Bookings that cannot be attended must be canceled no later than 60 minutes prior to the start time. Failure to cancel your Booking may result in a membership suspension and a loss of booking privileges.
  • In the event that UCLA Recreation cancels your Booking for any reason, you will not be issued a make-up Booking, membership extension, or any compensation.

Additional Member Services

Locker Rentals

Pricing:

  • $15/quarter
  • $5/month on Payroll Deduction*


Lockers are available on a first-come, first-serve basis and are not guaranteed. An active Recreation membership is required to purchase a locker rental.

*Please see UCLA Faculty/Staff section above for more information on enrolling in Recreation services using Payroll Deduction.

Locker Rental Request Process: 

  1. Submit Locker Rental Request Form. 
  2. The Sales Team will review requests in the order they were submitted within 3-5 business days. If approved, the Sales Team will assign you a locker and you will receive an email with instructions to purchase your locker ($15/quarter). 
  3. Log into your Recreation account and submit online payment within 5 business days of locker assignment or you are subject to lose your locker rental. Due to a high volume of requests, we cannot guarantee your locker after the payment deadline. 

The maximum locker duration is one quarter. Upon purchase, you will see an expiration date of June 10, 2022 for spring quarter.

Locker Rental Purchase Instructions:  

  1. Log in to your Recreation account: https://secure.recreation.ucla.edu/MemberDetails#Lockers 
  2. On the left column, click on “Locker Assignment” 
  3. Use the “Renew” button to purchase your locker for $15.00/quarter. Please select the correct quarter for your purchase and confirm the dates are accurate in your cart. 
  4. Proceed through the checkout process  

Note: You will see the locker number and combination once you are in the shopping cart. This information is not printed on the receipt, and cannot be emailed to you. If you ever need to retrieve this information in person, please stop by the facility front desk with your identification and the front desk staff will assist you. 

North Pool locker rooms have not yet reopened and therefore are not available for rental at this time.

Membership & Locker Refund Policies 

Monthly Recreation memberships are non-refundable; no exceptions.  

Quarterly Recreation memberships are eligible for a prorated refund for one of the following reasons, require documentation, and are subject to a cancellation fee of $25:   

  • Termination of employment  
  • Transfer off-campus  
  • Formal leave of absence and sabbaticals   
  • Changes in medical condition resulting in physical limitations  

Facility capacities are limited in certain facilities and areas. Availability and Bookings are not guaranteed and do not qualify for membership refunds, discounts or extensions. Facility and campus closures may occur as needed, and membership extensions are not offered. 

Payroll Deduction Cancellation: Payroll Deduction is perpetual and will continue until canceled. Payroll Deduction must be canceled in writing via the online form (see below) and timestamped by the 10th day of the month to cancel your deduction for the following pay period. Employees must cancel payroll deductions prior to going on temporary leave or ending appointment with UCLA, or you will automatically get additional charges (non-refundable) upon return to any UC location.

Member must submit the Cancellation Request form for review:  https://uclacampuslife.formstack.com/workflows/cancellation 

Program & Class Refund Policies 

Classes and Activities Refund Policy

Refund requests received by Noon (PST) 2 business days prior to the first class or meeting receive a 90% refund and requests received prior to the 2nd class meeting may be refunded 50%. Refunds are not available after the 2nd class meeting.

Activity and Fitness Passes (GroupX In-Person, GroupX Virtual, BStrong, Martial Arts)  
There are no refunds for quarterly activity passes once the first class begins.  

Marina Aquatic Center Refund Policy 
Refund requests received at least three business days prior to the first class meeting are eligible for a 90% refund or 100% transfer towards another in the same quarter. There are no refunds or transfers after the 1st class meeting. 

Fitness Class Transfer Policy 
Transfer requests for FITWELL/Instructional Program classes receive a 100% credit towards another class if used in the same quarter, space permitting. 

Personal Fitness Training and Private Instruction 
If you choose to pay for sessions in advance, a refund may be issued for unused sessions less a $25 processing fee if the request is received by UCLA Recreation within 6 months of the purchase. Sessions expire 6 months after the date of purchase. 

Summer Camps 
All refund requests must be submitted in writing and received by UCLA Recreation by the dates listed below. 

  • Refund Amount 90%, Request Must be Received Before March 31st 
  • Refund Amount 75%, Request Must be Received Before April 1st - 30th 
  • Refund Amount 50%, Request Must be Received After May 1: No later than 15 business days before the purchased session begins 
  • Refund Amount 25%, Request Must be Received Before Within 15 business days of the purchased session 
  • No refund after the first Monday of session or week. If UCLA Recreation cancels a program, a full refund will be issued. All camp transfers are subject to a $25 transfer fee per session/week. 

Youth and Family Programs (Including Winter Camp and Spring Camp)
All refund requests must be submitted in writing and received by UCLA Recreation by the dates listed below. 

  • Refund Amount 75%, Request Must be Received 31+ days before the first day of camp
  • Refund Amount 50%, Request Must be Received 11-30 days before the first day of camp
  • Not eligible for a refund if requested 0-10 days before the first day of camp 

If UCLA Recreation cancels a program, a full refund will be issued.

BHIP Continuation 
There are no refunds for BHIP Continuation paid up front per quarter or other short-term duration.
