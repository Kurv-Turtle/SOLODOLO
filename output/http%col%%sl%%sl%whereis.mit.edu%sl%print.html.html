You can also pick up a printed map on campus at the MIT Welcome Center, located at 292 Main Street in Kendall Square, conveniently located next to the Kendall/MIT MBTA subway station.

To obtain a customized version of this map for your publication, as an electronic file or printed version, please contact MIT Copytech at copytech@mit.edu.
