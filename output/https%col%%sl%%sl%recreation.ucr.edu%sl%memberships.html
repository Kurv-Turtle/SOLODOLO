Memberships

If you’re a UCR student, you’re already a member! The cost of your gym membership is included in your UCR tuition, which dates back to two student referendums passed in 1990 (currently adjusted to $20 per quarter) and in 2010 ($149 per quarter). We are conveniently located on campus and offer a wide array of amenities and services.

For further information, please contact Kim Stotts-Sanchez at kimberly.stotts@ucr.edu.
NOTE: The Student Recreation Center referendum fee is not optional. Opt out waivers or refunds are not available. All matriculated UCR students (undergrad & graduate alike) pay the $169.00 per quarter SRC fee. This dates back to two separate referendums, voted on and passed by students in 1989 (SRC North) and in 2010 (for the SRC Expansion).

ATTENTION: Due to logistical challenges and planned renovation/construction projects, some areas in the SRC will be unavailable for Summer 2022. Impacted areas may include the pool and the SRC North gym. Please plan your decision to purchase a membership accordingly.

In-person payment in the form of debit, credit and Apple Pay are accepted. The University does not accept cash.

Guest Memberships Fall/Winter/Spring/Summer Cost
UCR affiliate (eligible to become a SRC Member) $5 per day (valid the entire day for the SRC).

UCR Faculty/Staff/Retiree Memberships

Payroll deduction membership is available to all UCR faculty, staff and retirees. To register for SRC payroll deduction membership, please visit SRC South and complete a payroll deduction authorization form. The cost is $56.33 per month, and will be automatically deducted from your bi-weekly or monthly pay. The SRC payroll deduction membership is ongoing, with no expiration date. You may cancel your membership at any time. Complete a cancelation form at the SRC or email kimberly.stotts@ucr.edu and make your membership cancellation request by including an end date. The SRC membership fee is an after-tax deduction. 

NOTE: UCR staff, faculty and retirees may purchase an SRC membership through the UCRSRC mobile app or by clicking the button below.

2022 SRC Summer Special Access "40 for $80" One-Day Passes

  • You must be eligible for SRC principal membership to purchase forty (40) one-day passes for $80
  • Only sold un July and August
  • This is not an SRC membership
  • Entitles the pass holder to use open SRC facilities the entire day; can come multiple times on the day pass is redeemed if desired
  • Unused passes do not expire, they may be redeemed during the fall, winter and spring quarters
  • Passes are not transferable to another person, only the purchaser may redeem them
  • Day pass holders are not eligible to sponsor guests
  • Individuals may only purchase one bundle at a time; a new bundle may only be purchases after all 40 passes have been redeemed
  • Payable at the SRC south front counter; we accept credit, debit and apple pay

Pass holders received these benefits:

  • Access to and use of SRC exercise facilities and equipment
  • Access to group exercise classes offered on the day pass is redeemed
  • Access to pool and pool deck areas (pending availability)
  • Access to climbing wall

These services are not included:

  • Child Care Services
  • Personal Training Services
  • Chair Massage Services
  • Intramural Leagues

2022 Graduate Student Summer SRC Membership

  • Available only to UCR graduate students who are not able to enroll in summer classes but who must remain on campus for research and other academic responsibilities during the summer
  • The package provides full summer access and all member benefits for $14
  • The package is only available during the summer quarter when UCR graduate students' fellowship and grant funding is not generally available.
  • This program was the result of a collaboration between GSA, ASUCR and the Recreation Governing Board to respond to the unique needs of UCR graduate students on campus.

UC Recreation Reciprocity Program

Have you heard about “Reciprocity?” It’s actually a cool way to take advantage of the recreation departments at all of UC’s campuses. Your valid ID card works at gyms at UCLA, UC Irvine, UC San Diego, UC Santa Barbara, UC Berkeley, UC San Francisco, UC Davis and UC Merced. Best of all, it’s already included in your quarterly membership dues. And during the summer, if you live near another UC campus, you can join their facility by paying that campus’s summer rate. From San Francisco to San Diego, the UC Reciprocity program has you covered.

When traveling to other UC’s, faculty & staff who are members of UCR’s SRC may request a “Recreation Member Guest Pass” for use at any UC Recreation Center. Please contact Kim Stotts-Sanchez at kimberly.stotts@ucr.edu for more information regarding the UC Reciprocity Program.
