University of Massachusetts Global BA Legal Studies

2022 – 2023
Advising Information
College of the Canyons and University of Massachusetts Global

  • Legal Studies is an interdisciplinary, liberal arts major that engages in the meanings, values, practices, and institutions of law and legality. The curriculum examines how law shapes and is shaped by political, economic, and cultural forces.  The major is designed to stimulate critical understanding of and inquiry about the theoretical frameworks, historical dynamics, and cultural embeddedness of law.
    • In growing career fields such as law, public administration and organizational management, a strong working knowledge of the legal/constitutional order is expected.
    • A degree in legal studies provides the foundations necessary for success through courses that give you the theoretical, historical, social, and political foundations required to understand law in a democratic polity.
    • Crucial components of this degree include research, critical thinking, and argumentation.

  • Sheri Veges, Academic Advisor, sheri.veges@umassglobal.edu
