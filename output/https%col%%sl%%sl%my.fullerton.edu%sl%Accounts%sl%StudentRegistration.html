Welcome to California State University, Fullerton

To begin creating your campus account, sign in with your CWID (Campus-Wide ID) and registration PIN that you received in the email from CSU Fullerton.

If you cannot obtain your CWID and PIN, you may go to the CSUF Office of Admissions to get this information.

Note: You will need to wait two business days from the date you received your CWID and PIN before you can create your campus account.
