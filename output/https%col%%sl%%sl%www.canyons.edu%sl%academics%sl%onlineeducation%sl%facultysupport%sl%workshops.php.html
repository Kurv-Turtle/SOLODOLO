About the 'Design Thinking Series' 

Teaching is more than presenting a concept and asking students to demonstrate that they can repeat it back to us. Arguably, the most important goal of teaching is to spark that dynamic interplay of curiosity, exploration and engagement that results in learning. This series looks at the online learning environment from multiple perspectives, all aimed at giving you the ideas and tools to design effective instruction that engages your students and turns learning into success.

Click below for an overview of each of College of the Canyons' instructor certification training components

  • Canvas Certification

    To become 'Canvas Certified' at College of the Canyons, complete:

    • Intro. to Teaching with Canvas Self-paced online course.
      • Includes hands-on practice building Accessible content in Canvas and creating and grading assessments.
      • 5 Hours Flex credit
      • Course fulfills prequirement for OnlineLIVE Certification and Online Instructor Certification.

  • NEW: OnlineLIVE Instructor Certification

    To become 'OnlineLIVE Certified' at College of the Canyons, complete:

    • Canvas Certification (above)
    • Intro. to OnlineLIVE Instruction (online, synchronous course - Canvas & Zoom).
      • Includes live online lectures via Zoom and coursework in Canvas.
      • 9 Hours Flex credit

  • Online & Hybrid Instructor Certification

    To become 'Online Instructor Certified' at College of the Canyons, complete: 

    1. Canvas Certification (above)
    2. Section 508 Accessibility workshop
    3. Introduction to Online Instruction (five-week, 100% online course).

  • Refresh Online Instructor Certification

    'Refresh' Online Instructor Qualifications every three years by completing a self-paced online course (approx. two hours).

  • *Provisional Certification expires Dec. 2021.

    Provisional Online Instructor Certification Components*:

    1. Intro to Teaching with Canvas self-paced course ( 5 hours Flex credit - includes Accessibility training) 
    2. Keep Teaching Fall 2020 Self-paced course (4 hours Flex credit).
