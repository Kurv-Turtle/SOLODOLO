International Student Orientation

Welcome to UCR! We are looking forward to seeing you on campus. As a new international student, there are several orientation sessions you will need to attend.

Our office hosts the International Student Orientation (ISO), which is for all international students. There are also separate orientation sessions for undergraduate and graduate students that are hosted by other departments on campus.
