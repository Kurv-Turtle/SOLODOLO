Trainings

Online Training

Online training will take place for the foreseeable future. This training will be conducted via Zoom. There are three different trainings depending on your level. It is recommended that you reach out to your Organization Manager first regarding the plan for your site. 

  • Introductory Training - Two hour training meant for those new to Drupal. 
  • Refresher Course - One hour training meant for those who may have edited in the past would like a refresher.
  • Enhancing Your Site - One hour training workshop meant for those who have editing experience. Come with questions, this will be driven primarily by the people signed up.
