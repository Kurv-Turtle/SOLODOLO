COMMITTEE SERVICE INTEREST FORM


The Executive Committee of the Academic Senate must nominate faculty to serve on more than 40 committees and boards each year. Over 100 positions are vacated as faculty complete their terms, which range from 1 to 3 years. These committees and boards address a wide variety of issues, such as campus planning and resources, curriculum, student academic life and success, and faculty affairs.

In addition to helping shape the educational and professional policies and practices of the campus, committee service is a great way to meet faculty from other colleges, campus administrators, staff members, and especially student representatives.

If you have questions, please contact the Academic Senate Office at extension 3683. To access the Committee Service Interest Form, enter your Email and Password below and click SUBMIT.
