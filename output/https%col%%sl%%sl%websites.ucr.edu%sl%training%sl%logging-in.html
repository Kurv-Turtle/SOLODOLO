Logging In

To begin working on your site, you will need two pieces of information:

  • the URL of your site: Launched *.ucr.edu domain or Pantheon URL
  • your campus Net ID and password information.

Login Information

Your Organizational Manager will need to create your account on your site and provide you with this information. Once that's done, follow these steps to log in:

  1. Navigate to your site's URL.
    If not launched, use the Pantheon Site domain at https://live-ucr-*sitename*.pantheonsite.io 
    If launched, use the *sitename*.ucr.edu domain or pantheonsite.io domain (either one will update your site!)
  2. Add '/cas' to the end of the URL and hit the Return or Enter key. 
    Example: live-ucr-*sitename*.pantheonsite.io/cas or *site*.ucr.edu/cas
  3. CAS will be prompted, enter your Net ID and password. If you have a role within the site, you will be logged in and return to your site with the dashboard at the top.  

Log Out

To log out, click on the person icon next to your Net ID at the top. This will display the people toolbar. Select "Log out".
