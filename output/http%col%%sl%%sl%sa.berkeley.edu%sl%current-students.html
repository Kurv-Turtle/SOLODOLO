Current Students

Welcome to the Berkeley community. You join a culture characterized by self exploration, discovery, and diverse opportunities and thoughts. Find support in a network of scholars who encourage you to amplify your voice, challenge the status quo, and transform the world of tomorrow. You can create your unique academic adventure: develop your vision of success, learn from interpersonal experiences, and flourish in all aspects of your life and wellness. You are an ambassador of the excellence and conviction that distinguishes Berkeley students—on campus, in the community, and in shaping the future.
