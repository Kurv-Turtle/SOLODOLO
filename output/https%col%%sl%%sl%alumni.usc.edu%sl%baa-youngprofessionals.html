Social Media Groups

The Official USC Black Alumni Association Group

The Official USC Black Alumni Association Group connects and shares news about USC, and alumni achievements.  This group is open to all Black Trojans and the USC community.  Join here.

USC BAA Young Professionals Group

The BAA Young Professionals (BAAYP) is a network collective for the next generation of Black leaders. Open to anyone who graduated from USC in the last 15 years, BAAYP strives to create a supportive environment that encourages self-discovery, teaches collaboration and promotes Black Trojan excellence; the network also seeks to build strong ties between current students and alumni.

Members share professional advice and experience through BAAYP’s LinkedIn group; volunteer their time and talents at meet-ups or service projects; and fundraise for BAA scholarships.

Interested in joining BAAYP? Sign up today!
