Nurses in Transition
Mentor Program

We are proud to support the entry of all newly hired nurses into our Nurses in Transition Mentor Program. A few benefits include:

  • Ability to connect regularly with your assigned mentor
  • Participation in a 6 month structured program designed to improve performance and self-satisfaction
  • Complimentary professional development with access to over 24+ CE-tied webinars

We are honored to be a Magnet designated hospital system since 2011 and we have over 2,500 nurses who make it all possible.

New Graduate Nurse Transition to Practice Program

The New Graduate Nurse Transition to Practice Program is accredited with distinction as a Practice Transition Program by the American Nurses Credentialing Center’s Commission on Accreditation in Practice Transition Programs. This program is multi-faceted and designed to support our New Grads with a comprehensive transition-to-practice experience through enhanced clinical learning, patient simulation activities, hands-on skill training and so much more! There are seven, 4-hour classes held over a six month period and are facilitated by our incredible NRP Nurse Residency Program (NRP) dream team.

We hire new graduate nurses three times a year.

  • October – November for programs starting in February
  • March – April for programs starting in July
  • June – July for programs starting in October
