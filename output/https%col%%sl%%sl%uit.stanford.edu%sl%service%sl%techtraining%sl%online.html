Online Resources

Technology Training provides both instructor-facilitated and self-paced online training options for independent or remote learners.

​​
LinkedIn Learning

University and SLAC benefits-eligible staff, faculty and students have free and unlimited access to LinkedIn Learning. LinkedIn Learning is an online training library with over 9,000 courses aimed at helping learners develop their business, software, technology and creative skills.

Visit linkedinlearning.stanford.edu

ServiceNow Training

Available through STARS, ServiceNow Training familiarizes users with the ServiceNow environment and teaches fulfillers how to manage incidents and requests, changes, and KnowledgeBase article production.

View training options


Cloud Academy

Cloud Academy is a training platform for Amazon Web Services, Azure, and Google Cloud infrastructure with an expanding catalog of courses and learning paths. Members of the Stanford community can work directly with Cloud Academy to purchase a monthly or annual license. 

Enroll in Cloud Academy

Tech Briefings Video Archive

The Tech Briefings Video Archive contains complete recordings of the past year's tech briefings, which provide information about new and emerging technologies.

Visit techbriefings.stanford.edu

Technology Webinars

Technology Training offers live webinars that are STAP eligible and cover a breadth of applications and topics.
