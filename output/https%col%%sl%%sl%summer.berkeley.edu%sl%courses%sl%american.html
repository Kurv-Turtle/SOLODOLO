American Cultures Classes

All UC Berkeley students are required to complete one American Cultures class as part of their graduation requirement. These classes are designed to encourage students to analyze the racial, cultural and ethnic diversity of the United States. Students will learn how these complex issues influence the way we handle issues and problems as individuals, in the community, and in the country. Summer is an ideal time to take one of these important classes and continue to make progress towards your degree. Click on the link below to view more details from the Class Schedule.
