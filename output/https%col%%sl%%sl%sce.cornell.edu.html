Any person, any study, any time, any place

Cornell was founded as a place where any person could find instruction in any study. Spring, summer, fall, and winter. Online, on campus, or on safari in Tanzania. No matter what you're looking for, the School of Continuing Education (SCE) can help you access a Cornell education.
