Scheduled Maintenance for USC NetID Accounts Services (May 16)

Posted on May 11, 2022 by mendinue

From 2:00 a.m. to 3:00 a.m. on Monday, May 16, ITS will be performing scheduled maintenance on the USC NetID Account Services website to apply a database upgrade.

During this maintenance, faculty, students, and staff may be unable to activate their USC NetIDs, change their USC NetID passwords, reset a forgotten password, or update their secondary email addresses.

If your faculty, students, or staff encounter issues using this website to manage their USC NetID accounts, please have them call the ITS Service Desk (CSC) at 213-740-5555. The CSC is available by phone 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.
