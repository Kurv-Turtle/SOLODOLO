Audition for Amazon Prime's "The College Tour"

Amazon Prime’s "The College Tour" (a globally-streamed series) is coming to UC Riverside, to film a 30-minute episode about the things that make UCR special. They need approximately 10 students interested in sharing their stories and what they love about UCR. Check out clips from the campuses the show has already visited.

 

All current students and 2022 graduates are welcome to audition, but anyone chosen will need to attend the production shoot on the UCR campus during July 25-29.

 

Audition Details: Submit a self-recorded video (2-minute max), introducing yourself and the topics you’d like to discuss. We’re looking for a variety of topics, including interesting research, career development or internships, things you love about Riverside, student orgs and involvement, connecting with the Riverside community, your favorite faculty members, resource centers you’ve found incredibly useful, or anything about your unique journey to UCR.

Submit your audition video by June 17 (extended from June 10).

 

For more info, email: Adam Daniels (Enrollment Marketing Specialist, University Communications)
