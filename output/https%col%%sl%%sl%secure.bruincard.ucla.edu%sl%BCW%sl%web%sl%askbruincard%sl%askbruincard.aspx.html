Ask BruinCard

If you are a UCLA student, faculty, or staff or someone who has been been granted third party access, please sign in to MyUCLA to send your question through the Message Center.



For cardholder inquiries such as freezing a lost or stolen card, updating pin, or making a deposit, select the Cardholders option.

For inquiries on behalf of a cardholder that you parent or sponsor, select the Parents & Third Parties option.

For department inquiries such as application support, card holder privilege, and card production service questions, select the Department option.

For on- or off-campus merchant inquiries such as reader support, payment statements, or ordering additional readers, select the Merchant option.

To view a list of our Frequently Asked Questions, select the FAQ option.

Notice: A BruinCard will only be issued upon presentation of an unexpired government issued photo identification.
