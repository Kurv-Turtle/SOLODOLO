  • Organizational Charts Submittal Guidelines

When to Submit

  • Best practices suggest that you submit your organization chart on a quarterly basis in order to be current
  • You are welcome to submit updated organizational charts throughout the year as needed
  • Please send the organization chart to Judy Abbey - judyab@ucr.edu.

Creating the Chart

  • You are welcome to create your organization chart by using any application such as Visio, PowerPoint, Excel, etc.
  • Open and Save As to your computer in order to save your changes
  • Be sure to include your organization name as well as a revision date in the bottom right corner
  • Upon completion of the organization chart, create a PDF version and submit to Human Resources
