Consultations with GSI Center Staff

The GSI Center offers several kinds of consultations. All consultations are confidential.

For Graduate Students

  • Teaching consultations
  • Classroom observations
  • Consultations on teaching materials for the academic job search
  • Feedback on course syllabi

GSIs may request an appointment in advance by filling out the consultation request form.

GSIs are also welcome to drop by the office in 301 Sproul Hall to discuss immediate concerns in their teaching. The office is open 9–12 and 1–5, Monday through Friday.

For Faculty Members and Departments

Faculty members may wish to contact the GSI Center to discuss their work with GSIs as mentors and supervisors, 300-level pedagogy course instructors, or department Faculty Advisers for GSI Affairs. We are also available for consultation about departments’ reviews of their preparation of GSIs for teaching.

Faculty members wishing to schedule a consultation should contact the GSI Center via email: gsi@berkeley.edu.
