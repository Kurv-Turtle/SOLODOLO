Titan Connection

 

Students

Are you a CSUF student looking for a part-time on-campus job, internship, full time career or volunteer opportunity? Titan Connection is your access to hundreds of these opportunities just for Titans and much more. To access Titan Connection click on "Find a Job".

Find a Job

 

Recruiters

CSUF Titans are Career Ready. They come prepared with experience for the professional world and are eager to be your source of talent and pipeline of your organization's next generation of new leaders. If you are a recruiter looking to hire a talented Titan and promote your opportunities then make sure to click on "Post a job".

Post a Job

 

Schedule Appointment

Now you can schedule a Virtual Career Counseling appointment Online through Titan Connection!

Meet with a College Career Specialist or Career Coach Virtually on various topics including: graduate school, interview preparation, major/career exploration and many more. 

Schedule your appointment now using Titan Connection.

 

Note: When logging into Titan Connection, please select Calendar - Counseling Appointment from the left hand column and select, "New Appointment".  

NOTE: Effective May 31, 2022, For College of HHD and HSS, Scheduled Appointments (In-Person and Virtual) will now be provided through Titan Net. Appointments will no longer be accessed through Titan Connection for these two Colleges.
