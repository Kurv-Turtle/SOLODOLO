Activities and Events Open To All UCLA Students

Community.ucla.edu offers a collection of virtual and in-person events organized by student clubs and departments within the Division of Student Affairs.

You will find regularly offered activities like recreational classes, meditation sessions, and open mic nights on The Hill. You will also find campus-wide events like the Career Fair, Westwood Block Party, and Volunteer Day. Check-out the specialized filters to customize your view

Student Affairs is continuously changing, learning, and adapting to provide the best experience …we are where community happens.

Find the Support You Need

To help support you our services remain open and and accessible for all students.

Telehealth Services

Telehealth appointments are available for patient visits and are available through the ASHE Center Patient Portal.

 Therapy Services

Sign up for a teletherapy or in-person appointment with a CAPS clinician for a mental health assessment and options for appropriate care.

 Recreation and Wellness

Stay physically healthy by being an #ActiveBruin at home. Explore our collection of at-home and in-person classes, activities, and lessons.

 Student Resiliency

Stay motivated with virtual mindfulness, yoga, and other stress management resources.

 Basic Needs for Students

Updates on the Food Closet, Basic Needs services, and the food box drop-off program.

 Career Development

Connect with advisors and employers online to learn more about navigating career development in a virtual environment.

 Support for International Students

Get resources, support, and answers to frequently asked questions related to OPT, travel, F-1 and J-1 visas, and more.

 Student Support Line

If you’re feeling overwhelmed by the amount of information regarding COVID-19, please email us or call the Student Support Line at 310-825-3894 from 9am-7pm, Mon through Fri.
