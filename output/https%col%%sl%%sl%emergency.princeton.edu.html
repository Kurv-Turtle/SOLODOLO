Welcome to the Princeton University Emergency Management website. The information contained in this site provides the University community with guidance to help foster and focus the emergency preparedness efforts for the campus as a whole.  The University’s Emergency Management Group assembled information that you can review before an emergency or consult during an incident, including:

  • HOW TO PREPARE – Tips and tools to avoid or manage an emergency
  • WHAT TO DO – Actions to take during an emergency
  • STAY CONNECTED - Ways to stay informed before, during and after an event
