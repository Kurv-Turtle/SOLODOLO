CAP Benefit

  • Overview

Thanks to those who attended CalArtsX last Thursday, November 7th--our benefit auction to support CAP's work with over 8,000 public school children. The auction continues online here for just a few more days: featuring works by recent CalArts Alumni and CAP participants for sale at listed price! Your purchase helps to fund this vital program for current and future CalArtians.

Please email specialevents@calarts.edu or call 661-253-7727.
