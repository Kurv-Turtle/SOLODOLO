Hire Haas

Employer Resources

Hire Haas: In any year, but especially this year, we call on you to Hire Haas. If you are in the fortunate position to be hiring for full-time positions or internships, please share open positions for MBA students or graduates with the Career Management Group (CMG), and positions or internships for undergraduates with the Berkeley Career Center through Handshake, and specify a preference for Haas UG majors.

Berkeley Haas makes it easy to connect with a diversely talented, global alumni community united by a commitment to excellence and innovation. To connect with a CMG representative for help in posting your role, please email us.

Hire Haas!

  • Post a Job for Berkeley MBA students and alumni (for Berkeley Haas Alumni)
  • Post a Job for Berkeley MBA students and alumni (for non-Berkeley Haas Alumni)
  • Post a Job for Haas Undergraduate students and alumni
  • Post a Job to all Berkeley Haas Alumni in our official Alumni LinkedIn Group. Flag/tag it as [HIRE HAAS].
