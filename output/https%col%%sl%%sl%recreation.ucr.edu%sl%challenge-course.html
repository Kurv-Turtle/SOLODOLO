Challenge Course

The Challenge Course at the UC Riverside Student Recreation Center is available to campus departments, student organizations/groups and the entire community.

For general questions regarding our Challenge Course, please contact us at teambuilding@ucr.edu. 

CHALLENGE YOURSELF

Take time to sharpen your sense of self and your sense of direction at our Challenge Course!

Our course provides team building and leadership opportunities for all UCR students, organizations, departments, schools and businesses.

  • Request More Information

Challenge Course Package Options

Our outdoor challenges are customized for each group and are tailored to their specific goals. We're confident that your group will enjoy a supportive environment that is focused on fun, leadership and character development.

  • R'Team (Low Course)
  • R'Challenge (Low & High Course)
  • Challenge Course Climbing Wall

Program Length: 2.5 hours
Participants will attempt low-to-the-ground challenges using group effort. Personal reflection time and “team spirit” activities included.

Note: Rates are per person and include set-up, equipment cost and staffing.

Group Type Cost Per Person (2.5 Hours)
UCR Student Organizations/Groups $8
UCR Departments $12
Non-Campus Community $20

To reserve your experience, contact us at teambuilding@ucr.edu today!

Program Length: 4 or 6 hours
Effort – versus completion – is emphasized with our climbing wall, catwalks, giant swing and trapeze. Each gives a risky impression, but is as safe as any low-ground challenge. Achieve improved initiative and trust.

Note: Rates are per person and include set-up, equipment cost and staffing.

Group Type Cost Per Person (4 Hours) Cost Per Person (6 Hours)
UCR Student Organizations/Groups $12 $18
UCR Departments $18 $25
Non-Campus Community $34 $48

To reserve your experience, contact us at teambuilding@ucr.edu today!

Maximum Occupancy: up to 50 people
Climb on up for fun, fitness and confidence building (UCR student organizations and departments only). Expert staff, safety gear, climbing shoes and an extreme sense of accomplishment included.

Group Type Cost Per Person (2.5 Hours)
UCR Student Organizations/Groups/Departments $100

To reserve your experience, contact us at teambuilding@ucr.edu today!

Break Out of the Comfort Zone Programs

Maximize your organization’s confidence, communication and problem-solving skills with our “break out of the comfort zone” programs. All ages and levels welcome. 

Warm-ups

Games to overcome embarrassment and establish a safe learning environment.

Initiatives

Activities to challenge the mind and enhance teamwork.

Low and High Course

Activities to promote physical and mental challenge, creativity and team spirit.
