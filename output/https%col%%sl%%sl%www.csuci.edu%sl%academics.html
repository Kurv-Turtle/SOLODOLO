Academics

Student-centered. Transformative. That's CSUCI. Our more than 30 academic programs at CSUCI are committed to multicultural, interdisciplinary approaches, and providing you with hands-on, real-world experiences locally and internationally. Whether you're pursuing a Bachelor's or Master's degree or certification to advance your career, CSUCI will help you achieve your academic goals. Explore our programs by reviewing our list of courses in the Course Catalog or the academic programs below.
