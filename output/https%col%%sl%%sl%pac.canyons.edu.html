On sale Friday!

Tickets for the 2022/23 Season go on sale starting at 11 a.m. Friday, June 17. The new season features "Weird Al" Yankovic, The Doo Wop Project, Revisiting Creedence, Richard Marx, Naturally 7, Jon Secada, Perondi’s Stunt Dog Experience, and Lisa Loeb. We look forward to seeing you!

 

ADVISORY – Although patrons are no longer required to provide proof of COVID-19 vaccination or negative test result to attend performances, we strongly recommend that face masks be worn inside the venue.

The Center

Located on the campus of College of the Canyons, this stunning facility boasts a spectacular 7,500-square-foot proscenium stage with a state-of-the-art sound system and 886 seats, as well as the experimental Black Box Theater.
