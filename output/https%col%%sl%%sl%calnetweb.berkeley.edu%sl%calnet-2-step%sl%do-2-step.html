Do the 2-Step

All It Takes is Two Steps.

With 2-Step Verification, you log in as usual to CalNet (Step 1) and then verify your identity and approve access to your account at the Duo Security login window (Step 2).

Methods for Second Step Verification

You can choose one of these methods for the second step verification, depending on the verification devices you set up:

  • Duo Push (preferred method for smartphones and tablets)
    • Here's a quick video showing how easy the 2-step Push is!
  • Passcodes (for passcodes generated from smartphones, cellphones, and hardware tokens, as well as backup passcodes generated from CalNet Account Manager)
  • Touch ID (for macOS with Chrome)
  • Call Me (for landlines)
  • Simple Hardware Token (for users with no other second factor devices)

Do I Have to Verify Each Time I Log In?

You have the option of verifying just once a month. Once you have authenticated using 2-step, a window will pop up asking "Trust this browser?" Click “Yes, trust browser” and the browser will automatically remember you for 30 days. (Note: If during this time you use a new browser or clear your browser history, you'll have to reselect this option.)
