How You Can Make a Difference

Thank you for supporting International Affairs! Our mission is to cultivate conscientious global citizens and enhance the UCR community by facilitating meaningful international experiences through research, education, and cultural exchange.

We make it possible for UCR to welcome international students, host visiting researchers, and send UCR students to study, intern, or conduct research abroad. Our goal is to enhance UCR’s global impact by facilitating international collaboration, cutting edge research, and educational exchange.

Giving to International Affairs funds supports UCR’s global impact

Education Abroad Scholarship Fund

When you give to the Education Abroad Scholarship Fund, you help us to:

  • Fund and revitalize scholarships for exceptional students who want to learn abroad. 
  • Develop a fund for students who are abroad and dealing with emergencies.


International Affairs Fund

When you give to the International Affairs fund:

you help us create new programs, provide support for International Students and Scholars, as well as fund specific international initiatives.
