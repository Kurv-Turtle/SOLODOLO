Research

UCSB offers numerous opportunities for undergraduate students to get involved in research. We encourage our transfer students to engage in these opportunities that will provide them with a well-rounded university experience. Learn more about the:

  • Transfer Student Research Award (TSRA)
  • Faculty Research Assistance Program (FRAP)
  • Undergraduate Research and Creative Activities Grant (URCA)
  • Undergraduate Research Colloquium
    • Poster Colloquium
    • Digital Colloquium
  • Undergraduate Research Slam
  • and more!
