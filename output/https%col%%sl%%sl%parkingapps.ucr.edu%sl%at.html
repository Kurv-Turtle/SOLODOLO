REGISTER FOR R’COMMUTE PROGRAM

The R’Commute Program is an incentive program for faculty, staff and graduate students that elect not to purchase a monthly/quarterly/annual parking permit and instead use a sustainable commuting option. To enroll, click on "Login" to get started. Once signed in through CAS, click the "Register for R’Commute Program".

For program details and incentives available through our R’Commute Program programs, explore your options below:

  • Active Transportation
  • Carpool
  • Bus
  • Metrolink
  • Vanpool
  • Telework

REDEEM A 10-PACK PARKING PERMIT

If this is your first time redeeming a 10-pack parking permit, click on "Login" to get started. Once signed in through CAS, click on "Access 10-Pack Permits/Daily Web Permits".

To get started, please login via UCR CAS.
