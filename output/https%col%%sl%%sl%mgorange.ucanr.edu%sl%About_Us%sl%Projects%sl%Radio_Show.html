UCCE Master Gardener Radio Show

‘In the Garden with UC Master Gardeners’

When: Thursday mornings, 8:30am – 9:30am

Where: KUCI, 88.9 FM or podcasts (listed below)

Join UC Master Gardeners every Thursday morning for garden tips, fun gardening projects, and interviews with horticulture experts on topics specifically geared to our Orange County gardens.

Podcasts will be available within a few days of the broadcast.

They are not currently available as a subscription.

The first show of each month features ‘What to do in the Garden this Month’.   Other shows feature interviews with UC experts on pest control and other topics, as well as local garden experts on roses, fruit trees, and composting to name a few.

Email us at ucceocmghotline@ucanr.edu or contact Garden Hotline if you have a topic or guest suggestion for a future show and or have any gardening questions.
